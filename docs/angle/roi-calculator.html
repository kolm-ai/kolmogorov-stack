Show the formulas (no magic)
  • tickets/year = members × tickets_per_member_per_year
  • deflected = tickets/year × deflection_rate
  • assisted = tickets/year − deflected
  • hours_deflected = deflected × (AHT_minutes / 60)
  • hours_saved_on_assisted = assisted × (AHT_minutes / 60) × AHT_reduction
  • agent_hours_saved = hours_deflected + hours_saved_on_assisted
  • gross_labor_saving = agent_hours_saved × loaded_cost_per_agent_hour
  • kolm_cost_year_1 = kolm_run_cost_monthly × 12 + implementation_cost
  • net_savings_year_1 = gross_labor_saving − kolm_cost_year_1
  • payback_months = (implementation_cost) / ((gross_labor_saving / 12) − kolm_run_cost_monthly)
Conservative defaults justified:
  • Deflection rate starts at 35%; specialist models trained on captured tickets typically reach 40-60% within a quarter of re-distill cycles. We hold the model to 35% in this view.
  • AHT reduction on assisted tickets is 22%; published draft-assistant studies see 30-50%. We use 22% to keep the bar low.
  • Loaded cost per agent-hour is $55/hr fully loaded. If you use a higher BPO rate, the savings scale linearly.
  • kolm run cost is hardware + power + a monthly re-distill batch. No per-token markup; the gateway is Apache-2.0 software.
  • This estimator is for tier-1 specialist deflection only. It does not credit downstream effects (member NPS lift, fewer manual chart reviews, lower handoff loss). Real ROI is typically higher.
