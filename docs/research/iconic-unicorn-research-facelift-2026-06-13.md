## What The Unicorns Prove

The best competitors are not winning through vague "AI platform" language. They show a concrete operating object:

- Workato: governed agent orchestration over enterprise applications.
- MuleSoft: universal API, LLM, and agent management.
- Airbyte: live context store and action layer for agents.
- Fivetran: reliable, governed data movement at scale.
- Confluent and OpenLineage: event and lineage substrate.
- Akto: agent/MCP discovery, attack posture, and runtime guardrails.
- Vanta and Drata: trust, compliance, and evidence operations.
- Langfuse: traces, evals, prompt management, and OpenTelemetry-native observability.
- Pioneer: closed-loop model improvement with regression constraints.

## Kolm Product Requirement

Kolm must not claim to be a better version of every specialist. The sharper wedge is:

Capture any credible API/data signal, keep policy before interpretation, compile readiness-gated artifacts, and export signed proof to the systems enterprises already use.

This means the public site must show:

- Enterprise systems as source, sink, lineage, security, and GRC nodes.
- Kolm as the source-to-proof control kernel.
- Outputs as canonical event envelopes, readiness-gated `.kolm` artifacts, and enterprise proof exports.
- Pioneer-style improvement loop without claiming benchmark superiority until the public benchmark gate closes.
- Image-2 design language: paper grid, black type, green proof accent, dark technical panels, no generic purple/blue SaaS hero.
