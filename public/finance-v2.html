What compliance and risk teams tell us.

Concrete pains we hear from BSA officers, model-risk leads, and platform engineering.

Customer data leakage

  • GLBA and CCPA forbid sharing PII with third-party models
  • FFIEC IT Handbook requires data-flow attestation
  • SaaS LLM vendors silently train on your prompts

Model risk management (SR 11-7)

  • Examiners want explainability and challenger models
  • Vendor opacity prevents independent validation
  • Need reproducible eval on frozen seed splits

SAR-narrative quality

  • FinCEN expects structured rationale for each filing
  • Hallucinated transaction summaries trigger re-filings
  • Bespoke red-flag taxonomy per institution
