Controls your authorizing official already reads.

aligned

Control mapping against FedRAMP Moderate baseline is shipped. kolm artifacts run inside customer FedRAMP-authorized boundaries today (BYO IaaS). 3PAO sponsor conversations are open.

NIST AI RMF

readiness path

CUI handling stays inside the artifact · no egress at runtime. CMMC L2 self-assessment templates and L3 readiness checklists ship with Enterprise deployments.

at rest & in transit
