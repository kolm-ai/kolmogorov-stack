How this ships

Items 3 + 4 (marketplace listing + this landing page) are live now. Items 1 + 2 (vertical capture-lake fingerprint + per-vertical foundation pre-train) are blocked on (fingerprinting pipeline) and (cross-namespace transfer math). The marketplace stub registers with pending_distill:true and kscore:null — we never paint a fake K-Score on an uncompiled student.

The pipeline once + land:

  1. Fingerprint your team's capture lake by vertical — per-namespace token-bigram sketch, scoped to vertical:'code', with secrets redacted before fingerprinting.
  2. Pre-train a 7B base student against the merged of code corpora.
  3. Compile to kolm-code-7b.kolm with sha256-pinned bytes, signed receipt, real K-Score eval against your team's PR-review holdout.
  4. Publish to marketplace — replaces the pending_ stub atomically.
