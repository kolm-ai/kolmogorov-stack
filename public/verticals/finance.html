How this ships

Items 3 + 4 (marketplace listing + this landing page) are live now. Items 1 + 2 (vertical capture-lake fingerprint + per-vertical foundation pre-train) are blocked on W757 (fingerprinting pipeline) and W715 (cross-namespace transfer math). The marketplace stub registers with pending_distill:true and kscore:null — we never paint a fake K-Score on an uncompiled student. Audit bar requires real eval against a held-out auditor-graded test set.

The pipeline once W757 + W715 land:

  1. Fingerprint your firm's capture lake by vertical (W757) — per-namespace token-bigram sketch, scoped to vertical:'finance', with PII/PAN redacted before fingerprinting.
  2. Pre-train a 7B base student against the merged W751-fingerprint of finance corpora (W715 cross-namespace transfer for similar-firm lift).
  3. Compile to kolm-finance-7b.kolm with sha256-pinned bytes, signed receipt, real K-Score eval against the per-firm holdout (auditor-graded).
  4. Publish to marketplace — replaces the pending_ stub atomically.
