01 What your team gets

Four things go wrong with scattered AI. kolm fixes all four.

See it

Every call, by person and team.

Point any tool at kolm and watch a live feed of who asked what, which model, and how much it cost. The visibility scattered usage never gave you.

Control it

Cost, access, and PII in one place.

Meter spend per team, set who can use which providers, and redact sensitive data before it leaves your boundary. You set the rules.

Own it

Models trained on your own work.

kolm turns the work your team repeats into a model you own and run cheaply. Light runs finish in minutes; intense runs train on cloud GPUs. You keep the weights.

Prove it

A signed record for every call.

SSO, SCIM, and RBAC plus a verifiable receipt on every call, so your security and finance teams can answer "what AI touched our data" with evidence.
