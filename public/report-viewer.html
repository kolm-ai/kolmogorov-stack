01 / How to read this page

      

    

      
The page is a lens. The JSON is the source.

      
Nothing on this page is the evidence. The signed JSON is. The viewer verifies it in your browser, then renders it without editing it. Two terms you will meet in the document: the evidence tier (how the logs were obtained)The evidence tier grades how the underlying logs were obtained: grade A is first-party capture by kolm, grade B is your hash-chained logs, grade C is plain vendor logs. It rates the source, not the result., and the posture grid (the eight-control status board)The posture grid is the board of eight controls. Each tile is grey until evidence supports it, then turns green on a pass or an earth tone when findings map to it. below the masthead.

    

    

      

        
Grey by default

        
Color is earned

        
A control tile only earns color when evidence supports it: green when it passes, earth tones when findings map to it. No evidence either way reads grey as insufficient evidence, never as a pass.

      

      

        
No composite score

        
Rollups stay rollups

        
The readiness percentage is one labeled line, not a hero number. A single percentage cannot carry a permission posture, an egress map, and an injection battery without flattening them.

      

      

        
The paywall is the signature

        
Findings are never blurred

        
A free Scan renders complete, with a watermark inside the signed bytes. The paid report is the same audit re-signed without the watermark. No locked sections, no hidden findings.

      

    

    
Scope is contractual. Permission posture, redaction and audit-trail integrity are assessed. Injection is tested and reported, not warranted.
