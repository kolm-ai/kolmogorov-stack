What partners and GCs tell us.

Concrete pains we hear in firm and in-house counsel evaluations.

Privilege risk in vendor prompts

  • SaaS LLM logs are discoverable in litigation
  • Outside-counsel matter cannot leave the tenant
  • Privileged communications need cryptographic chain-of-custody

Discovery and retention

  • FRCP 26(b) imposes preservation duties on AI workflows
  • Vendor retention policies conflict with hold orders
  • No clean way to delete a single matter from a hosted model

Bespoke matter workflows

  • Contract review, M&A diligence, lease abstraction all differ
  • General LLMs miss firm-specific style and precedent
  • Associates need turn-around in minutes, not weeks
