01The pain

What partners ask the
GC before signing.

Three concrete blockers we hear from AmLaw 200 GCs and litigation-tech leads.

Privilege

Vendor prompt logs threaten privilege.

A frontier API call against a draft brief can be argued as third-party disclosure. kolm runs no third party at inference time. The artifact is local.

Conflicts wall

Matter data cannot mix across teams.

Ethical walls require per-matter isolation. kolm compiles a per-matter artifact, signed and tenant-scoped, with a receipt chain that names the matter.

eDiscovery

Every prompt may be discoverable.

Opposing counsel will subpoena the vendor. kolm keeps prompts and responses on the firm's WORM store under your existing retention schedule.
