01 / Shipped

What's live now.

2026 · Junesite Site rebuilt around verifiable evidence A ground-up redesign around verifiable evidence: a live in-browser verifier on the homepage, the report anatomy, and the four-pillar check catalog. Everything centers on a signed artifact you can check yourself.
2026 · Juneverifier Open offline verifier A dependency-free WebCrypto Ed25519 verifier, shipped open. The browser widget, CLI, and library share one canonicalization. Paste a report and the check runs in-page, with a tamper demo to prove altered evidence can't pass.
2026 · Juneframework Agent Security Readiness (ASR) checklist Published openly under CC0: the six-control readiness checklist for agentic products, each control mapped into SOC 2, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, and the OWASP LLM & Agentic Top 10.
2026 · Q1log Transparency log + inclusion proofs Every report enters an append-only, hash-chained Merkle log and ships with a per-report inclusion proof, so a report can be shown to have been logged and never quietly replaced.
