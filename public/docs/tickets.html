KOLM-102Shipped

Enterprise plan inquiry funnel.

Goal

Capture enterprise leads with a structured intake at /enterprise/inquiry that posts to /v1/lead/enterprise and emails sales@kolm.ai.

Why now

Previously the /enterprise CTA routed to mailto:. Lost about 40 percent of intent signal because the lead did not know what to write. Structured intake closes that.

Acceptance
  • Form with seven required fields plus optional notes (company, role, employees, vertical, intended use, target start, email; notes optional).
  • POSTs to /v1/lead/enterprise; rate-limited to 5/hour/IP.
  • Persists to in-process enterpriseLeads array; admin GET /v1/lead/enterprise/:id for triage.
  • Emails sales@kolm.ai via Resend with structured payload and Reply-To set to the lead's email.
  • Inline success message with Calendly link and links to /security, /baa, /subprocessors.
Estimate 2d
Owner frontend + backend/router
KOLM-109Shipped

Verifiable receipt rendering at /r/:hash.

Goal

Public URL renders any receipt by hash with full chain verification, no auth required.

Why now

External review noted that receipts are claimed but no one outside the tenant can see one. A public receipt URL is social proof plus an audit trail external observers can verify.

Acceptance
  • GET /r/:hash returns rendered HTML showing receipt fields, Ed25519 receipt chain status (valid or broken), ring depth, signed-at timestamp, source artifact hash.
  • Tenant identity redacted by default unless the customer opts public.
  • OG card auto-generated for share previews.
Estimate 2d
Owner frontend + backend/router
KOLM-105Shipped

Goal

Clarify that customer opt-in subprocessors (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) flow down per tenant via the customer's contracted BAA with that vendor, not via kolm.

Why now

05contract · how to read this page

Tickets are aspirational.

Dates and scopes change as the team learns. The ticket is the contract; estimates are not. We move the date before we move the scope.

If a ticket here is load-bearing for your evaluation, write dev@kolm.ai and we will tell you plainly whether it will land in the window you need. Sponsored tickets jump the queue and ship under a public acknowledgement on the hall of fame if the contributor opts in.

For shipped work see the changelog. For directional bets that are not yet tickets see the roadmap. For paid bounties open to anyone see /security.
