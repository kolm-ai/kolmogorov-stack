kolm federated

Decision-aggregation federation: share hash-only approval rows across opted-in tenants. Distinct from gradient federation in kolm fl.

Usage

kolm federated

Flags

FlagDefaultDescription
opt-insubOpt this tenant in.
opt-outsubOpt out.
peerssubList federated peers.
sharesubShare approval-row hashes (never raw input/output).
aggregatesubPull noised per-decision counts from peers.
auditsubShow the federation audit log.

Examples

kolm federated opt-in
kolm federated share --namespace inbox
kolm federated aggregate --namespace inbox

Notes

Approval hashes are sha256(namespace+input+decision). Aggregates use Laplace noise ε=1.0. Raw text never leaves your tenant boundary.
