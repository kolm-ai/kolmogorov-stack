kolm ship

End-to-end deploy verb: takes a recipe or artifact, runs gate checks, signs, and ships to your configured runtime.

Usage

kolm ship <recipe.toml|artifact.kolm> [--target <slug>] [--region <slug>]

Flags

FlagDefaultDescription
requiredRecipe (will be made first) or pre-built artifact.
--target cudaCompile target.
--region us-east-1Deploy region (when shipping to managed runtime).
--byocoffShip to your BYOC cluster instead of managed runtime.

Examples

kolm ship ./recipes/support-bot.toml
kolm ship ./out/recipe.kolm --byoc

Notes

Pairs with kolm make. ship is the last-mile deploy; make is the build pipeline.
