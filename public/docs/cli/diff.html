kolm diff

Diff two artifacts, two capture exports, or two eval runs.

Usage

kolm diff   [--kind ]

Flags

FlagDefaultDescription
requiredTwo paths to compare.
--kind autoAuto-detected from extension. Force with this flag.

Examples

kolm diff ./v1.kolm ./v2.kolm
kolm diff ./eval-friday.jsonl ./eval-monday.jsonl --kind evals

Notes

For artifacts, diff prints metadata + k-score delta + size delta. For evals, prints per-prompt delta and aggregate regression. Exit 1 on regression.
