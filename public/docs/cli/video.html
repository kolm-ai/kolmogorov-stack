kolm video

Capture and run video through the multimodal pipeline. Frame-extract + per-frame VLM analysis.

Usage

kolm video <capture|extract> <file.mp4|webm> [--every <s>]

Flags

FlagDefaultDescription
capture-Record a video capture into the corpus.
extract-Extract frames + per-frame caption without storing.
requiredVideo file.
--every 1Frame extraction interval in seconds.

Examples

kolm video capture ./demo.mp4
kolm video extract ./demo.mp4 --every 5

Notes

Heavy work runs in workers/video-redact/. Faces and license plates are redacted by default.
