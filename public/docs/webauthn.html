kolm.ai · authentication

How passkey authentication works.

kolm.ai supports WebAuthn passkeys for sign-in and as a second factor. Passkeys are phishing-resistant by design: the private key never leaves your device.

Enroll a passkey

  1. Sign in to your account at /account.
  2. Open Security & 2FA.
  3. Click Add a passkey. Your browser prompts for the authenticator (Touch ID, Windows Hello, YubiKey, etc.).
  4. Name the passkey (for example, MacBook Touch ID). It is now active.

Supported authenticators

  • Platform authenticators: Touch ID, Face ID, Windows Hello.
  • Roaming authenticators: YubiKey, Solo, Google Titan, any FIDO2 hardware key.
  • Synced passkeys: iCloud Keychain, Google Password Manager, 1Password, Bitwarden.

Recovery

Enroll at least two passkeys (one platform, one hardware key) so a lost device does not lock you out. The dashboard also issues 10 one-time backup codes when 2FA is enabled.
