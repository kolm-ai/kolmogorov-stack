kolm.ai · cookbook

End-to-end recipes for production AI.

The cookbook is a curated set of full-pipeline walkthroughs. Each recipe shows capture, compile, eval gates, and deployment for a real task. Browse the full set at /docs/cookbook.

Featured recipes

  • SOAP redactor: HIPAA-grade PHI removal from clinical notes.
  • PHI redactor: structured PHI removal from free-form text.
  • Intake triage: structured extraction from free-form patient intake.
  • Healthcare vertical: the full healthcare pipeline.
  • Finance vertical: KYC, AML narrative review, document QA.
  • Legal vertical: privileged-document redaction, contract clause routing.

Browse by stage

  • Quickstart: the 5-minute first-recipe walkthrough.
  • Capture: record real traffic into a namespace.
  • Distill: turn captures into a recipe.
  • Compile: bake a recipe into a signed .kolm artifact.
  • Replay: side-by-side diff against the teacher.
