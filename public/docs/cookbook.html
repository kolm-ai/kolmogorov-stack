kolm.ai · cookbook

End-to-end recipes for production AI.

Thirty-five full-pipeline walkthroughs. Each one captures real traffic, distills it into a recipe, compiles a signed .kolm artifact, and shows the eval gates that decide whether it ships. Pick the closest shape to what you actually do - the workflow is the same regardless.

Start here

  • Recipe from observations - the canonical capture → distill → compile loop, end-to-end in one page.
  • Verifier from examples - turn known-good outputs into the eval gate that decides ship/no-ship.
  • k-score explainer - what the score on every artifact means and how to read it.

Developer productivity (7)

  • PR review - pre-merge review comments grounded in your team's actual past PRs.
  • Bug spotter - flag likely-broken hunks at diff time using your repo's bug history.
  • Test generator - generate test scaffolds aligned with your existing test style.
  • Refactor - apply codebase-specific refactor patterns deterministically.
  • Docstring - fill missing docstrings in your house style from function signatures + bodies.
  • Type hint - backfill type hints across an untyped codebase using observed call sites.
  • Feature spec from issue - expand a tracker issue into a spec your team would actually write.

Operations & incidents (5)

  • Incident summarizer - five-line incident summaries grounded in your runbook style.
  • Runbook step - pick the next runbook step from current alert + recent ops history.
  • On-call page classifier - route pages to the right team using your historical paging signal.
  • Log grep - natural-language → structured log query, compiled against your schema.
  • Recall namespace tagger - auto-tag captures into the right namespace for downstream distill.

Finance & compliance (2)

Support & communications (5)
