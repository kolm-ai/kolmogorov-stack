Teacher council.

A single teacher leaves its fingerprint on the student — same refusal patterns, same blind spots, same style. A council of three teachers plus a judge from a different lineage breaks the fingerprint. The student learns from consensus, the judge breaks ties, and the artifact ships less correlated with any one frontier provider.

The Trinity recipe

The default council shipped with kolm distill recipes is what we call Trinity:

RoleModelWhy
Teacher AClaude OpusLong-form reasoning, conservative refusals, strong calibration.
Teacher BGPT-4oStrong instruction-following, distinct error mode from Claude.
Teacher CDeepSeek V3Different training lineage, often disagrees with both A and B on edge cases.
JudgeGemini-2.5-ProYet another lineage; breaks tied labels by selecting the most-grounded option.

Trinity-500 reached 96.5% asks-1Q + 100% judge-clarify + 100% judge-on-policy at 1.24s/210 chars on the support benchmark — matching gpt-4o-mini at half the response length.

How labels merge

  1. Each teacher independently produces a labelled response for every capture.
  2. The judge scores each label against the original request, returning a per-label rubric score.
  3. If two or more teachers agree above the agreement threshold, their consensus is the training target.
  4. If teachers split, the judge's highest-scored label is the training target and the disagreement is logged for review.
  5. Captures where the judge scores every label below the floor are excluded from training and flagged for human inspection.

Cost discipline

Council costs scale linearly with the corpus. The CLI keeps a running tab and the wizard surfaces it before the run starts:

kolm distill --recipe trinity --captures 1832
# council cost forecast:
#   claude-opus    1832 * $0.018  = $32.98
#   gpt-4o         1832 * $0.012  = $21.98
#   deepseek-v3    1832 * $0.004  = $7.33
#   gemini-pro     1832 * $0.006  = $10.99  (judge)
# total: $73.28
# proceed? [y/N]

The API costs are billed against the keys you provide in /account/api-keys. kolm never proxies your teacher traffic.

Bring your own council

Any combination of teachers is configurable in spec.toml; the only hard constraint is that the judge must come from a different lineage than every teacher (the recipe linter enforces this). See spec.toml docs.
