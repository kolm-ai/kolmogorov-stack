Guarantees

What every regulated buyer asks for.

Every kolm install ships these by default. Not a roadmap. Not a paywall. Apache-2.0, verifiable in your VPC.

  • Tenant isolation

    physically isolated · not just permissioned

    Per-tenant data fences enforced at the storage layer. Cross-tenant reads are not a permission check; they are physically impossible. Verified by tests on every release.

  • Signed receipts

    Ed25519 · public CID

    Every artifact and every distill run produces an Ed25519-signed receipt with a public CID. Your auditor verifies independently. No vendor portal required.

  • BYOC or on-prem

    no outbound calls · your KMS

    Helm chart, Docker Compose, and a bare-metal install script. Runs entirely in your VPC. Your KMS, your bucket, your audit trail.

  • Real-time auditability

    SIEM-ready · CID-linked

    Per-request immutable audit log streamed to your SIEM (Splunk, Datadog, Elastic). Each entry references the receipt that produced it. Full chain of custody.

Deployment patterns

Pick the topology that matches your risk model.

Same product. Same artifacts. Different placement.

  1. 01 · SaaS multi-tenant

    Hosted on kolm.ai

    The fastest path. Tenant-isolated database, regional residency (US, EU, APAC), SOC 2 controls applied to the shared infrastructure. Most teams start here, then graduate.

    • 5-minute setup, no infra
    • Same uptime SLA as the Pro plan
    • Optional VPC peering (Enterprise)
  2. 02 · SaaS dedicated

    Hosted, but single-tenant

    Your own database, your own queue, your own region. We operate it; you own the data plane. Adds a dedicated VPN for ingress and your KMS for signing keys.

    • Dedicated database, dedicated workers
    • Bring-your-own KMS (AWS / GCP / Azure)
    • Custom domain, custom TLS
  3. 03 · BYOC

    Your cloud, our control plane

    The data plane runs in your AWS, GCP, or Azure account. We operate the control plane (deploys, upgrades, paging). No customer data leaves your perimeter.

    • Private subnets, your VPC, your IAM
    • Outbound to control plane is opt-in
    • You can pull the plug and keep running
    helm install kolm kolm/byoc \
  --set tenant=acme \
  --set region=us-east-1 \
  --set kms.arn=arn:aws:kms:...
  4. 04 · On-prem

    Your data center, your control plane

    Kubernetes or bare metal. No outbound calls. Upgrades shipped as signed bundles you verify before applying. Used by health systems, governments, and defense integrators.

    • Helm chart, Docker Compose, RPM/DEB
    • Signed upgrade bundles via SSH or USB
    • Detached license model, offline activation
    kolm install --on-prem \
  --bundle kolm-2026-05-22.tar.zst \
  --signature kolm-2026-05-22.sig
  5. 05 · Air-gap appliance

    Single-host, zero outbound

    A signed appliance image (qcow2 or OVA) that runs in a SCIF or PSE-zoned host. Boots offline, ships with a pre-loaded model registry, signed by us, verified by you.

    • OVA, qcow2, or raw disk image
    • Sealed model registry, offline updates
    • FedRAMP-friendly hardening profile

Identity & access

Identity that your IT team already runs.

Standards, not adapters. SAML 2.0 against any IdP, SCIM 2.0 against any directory, OAuth/OIDC for human sessions, signed JWTs for service-to-service. No proprietary glue.

  • SAML SSO

    Okta / Azure / Google / Ping / Auth0

    Tested against Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, OneLogin, Ping, JumpCloud, Auth0. Metadata published at /v1/sso/saml/metadata.

  • SCIM provisioning

    SCIM 2.0 · JIT

    SCIM 2.0 endpoints for user and group lifecycle. Service-provider config at /v1/sso/scim/ServiceProviderConfig. Just-in-time provisioning supported.

  • OIDC for humans

    OAuth 2.1 · PKCE

    OAuth 2.1 / OIDC for the CLI device flow and the dashboard. PKCE everywhere. Refresh-token rotation by default.

  • Signed JWT for services

    per-namespace · mTLS optional

    Per-namespace API keys, signed JWTs for service-to-service, mTLS available on enterprise plans. Keys rotate via SCIM lifecycle events.

  • Audit trail

    JSON / CEF · SIEM-ready

    Every auth event is in the audit log with the originating IP, IdP attributes, and the resulting tenant/namespace scope.

  • Break-glass

    escrow · dual-control · forced rotate

    Optional break-glass account with key escrow at your custodian, dual-control activation, and forced rotation after use. Required for regulated tenants.

Real customer questions

Procurement questions and our straight answers.

The actual exchange from the last twenty enterprise reviews, deduplicated.

Where is customer data stored?
Captures, distill runs, and registry artifacts live in your selected region (US, EU, or APAC). On BYOC and on-prem, "your region" is whatever bucket you configured. Metadata (org name, billing) lives in the US control plane. The full storage map is in the DPA appendix.
Do you train on our data?
No. Captured payloads are encrypted at rest with a per-tenant key. We never read your captures, never use them for model training, and never share them. The CLI has a --do-not-train flag (default on) and the gateway honors X-Kolm-DoNotTrain: 1 headers end-to-end.
How do we audit what your service does on our behalf?
Every request emits an audit log entry with the receipt CID. We ship a Splunk app, a Datadog integration, and a CEF formatter for arbitrary SIEMs. The audit log is append-only and signed; replay is detectable.
What if you get acquired or go out of business?
The product is Apache-2.0. The .kolm artifact format is open. The CLI runs offline. Your captures and distilled models keep working on your own infrastructure without any kolm-operated service. We additionally offer source escrow through Iron Mountain for enterprise contracts.
Can we self-host upgrades to avoid network egress?
Yes. Upgrades are shipped as signed bundles (tar.zst with a detached Ed25519 signature). Apply with kolm upgrade --bundle path --signature path. Verify the signature before applying; the CLI refuses to apply an unverified bundle.
What is your SLA?
99.9% monthly uptime on Pro, 99.95% on Enterprise SaaS, BYOC and on-prem have no kolm-side SLA (the product runs in your cloud). Credit schedule is in the MSA. Twelve months of status history at status.
How are signing keys handled?
SaaS: per-tenant Ed25519 keys stored in our HSM, rotated quarterly, never exported. BYOC: keys in your KMS (AWS KMS, Google Cloud KMS, Azure Key Vault). On-prem: keys on your HSM or YubiKey-backed file. The kolm process never sees the private key in plaintext; signing is delegated.
Do you have a vulnerability disclosure program?
Yes. security@kolm.ai, PGP key at /.well-known/security.txt. Triage SLO is 1 business day; remediation depends on severity. Hall of fame at /security/hof.
Can we run a pilot before signing?
Yes. 30-day pilot, in your own cloud, against your own data, with a written success scorecard at day 30. We do not charge for the pilot. We do not require a credit card. We do require a mutual NDA before access to the SOC 2 letter and pen test report.
What is the cost?
Enterprise SaaS starts at $4,000/mo for 5M gateway calls and 5 BYOC deployments. BYOC and on-prem are usage-priced on artifact builds and audit log volume; ask for a quote. We publish all our pricing on /pricing; the enterprise number is on this page so you do not have to email us.
