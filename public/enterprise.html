04 / Two clocks
04

Minutes for the scan. Days for a signature with a name on it.

An enterprise review stalls on two different clocks. We compress both, and we are clear about which one needs a person.

The machine clock

No human waits here

Permission read, audit-trail and egress checks, the injection battery, control mapping and signing are compute. Minutes to hours, re-run on every deploy, nobody in the loop.

The human clock

A name behind the result

A reviewer wants a person to stand behind the finding, not a machine that approved a machine. The co-signer reviews and signs in days, bounded by an SLA, not the open-ended wait of a from-scratch review.
