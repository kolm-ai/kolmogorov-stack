Session & account

Sign out clears local browser keys. Delete account deactivates the tenant: the API key is revoked, billing cancels, and the session clears. Concepts, versions, observations, invocations, compile jobs, and artifact zips are retained on the tenant row until you request a hard purge. For an operator-run purge, email dev@kolm.ai.
