02 / The lifecycle
02

One pipeline. Three moves.

Reproducible end to end: same logs, same result, same signature. Anyone you hand the report to can check it.

01 · Audit We examine the agent

From a log import or a sidecar proxy. Permissions, audit trail, data egress and injection resistance, read from what the agent actually did.

02 · Sign We seal the findings

Canonicalized and signed with Ed25519. The signature covers the exact bytes: inflate a score and the seal breaks. Every issuance enters an append-only log.

03 · Verify Your buyer checks it

Offline, in their own browser, against your public key. No account, no upload, no kolm server in the trust path.

04 / Two clocks
04

Minutes for the scan. Days for a name on it.

A review stalls on two different clocks. We compress both, and we are clear about which one needs a person.

The machine clock

No human waits here

Permission reads, trail and egress checks, and the injection battery are compute. They run in minutes to hours and re-run on every deploy. The free scan, the signed report and both continuous plans need no person.

minutes to hours · automated

The human clock

A name behind the result

Some reviewers want a person to stand behind the finding. A named co-signer reviews and signs alongside the automated result: days, bounded by an SLA, against the four to eight weeks a from-scratch review takes.

days · bounded by an SLA

05 / Continuous

Evidence that goes stale is not evidence.

A permission granted in January can still fire in August. Continuous re-attests weekly or on every deploy behind one live Trust link, so what your buyer checks is never older than your last release.

Versioned

Every report carries a spec version

When the agent changes, we re-run the affected checks and re-sign. You do not pay for a full audit again to keep the evidence current.

Provable history

The log shows nothing was swapped

Each issuance enters an append-only Merkle log (Ed25519 and SHA-256, RFC 6962 style), so a reviewer can confirm a report was issued when it claims and never quietly replaced.

Live Trust link

One link your buyer re-checks

It always resolves to the current signed report. No new email, no waiting on you, no human in the loop.
