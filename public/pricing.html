The economics

Why this looks different from every other LLM bill.

Most "AI platform" pricing scales with how much you use your model. Ours scales with how often you decide which model to use. After compile, the model is yours.

What you pay for

Gateway routing, capture storage, compile compute, registry hosting, the verify path. The control plane.

What you don't

Per-token inference on your .kolm. Self-hosting the runtime. Source code. SDKs. The CLI. Reading the docs.

Why the gateway costs anything

We run the cross-provider router, the capture ledger, the policy enforcer, the doctor, the registry. It costs us real money to keep them up.

Why inference is free

Because it runs on your hardware. We never touch the request. The .kolm is signed at build time and verified at load time. We can't bill what we can't see.

Try the stack. Ship a side project.

Pro $49 / mo · most teams start here

Ship a real product. Compile to spec.

Pro turns on the compile path. Once you have 100+ captures in a namespace, kolm compile "your job description" distills them into a student model, runs a bake-off against the teacher, scores it on a frozen eval, and ships a signed .kolm artifact. From that point on, you can run the artifact anywhere: cloud, laptop, browser, phone, VPC: and pay zero per-call.

500k calls is a lot of product surface. If each user interaction is 1 prompt, that's 16,000 daily-active users running a chat-style feature, or ~500 daily extracts on a 1000-doc/day pipeline. Most Pro accounts use 80–120k calls/month and never come close to the cap.

  • 500k calls / month: ~16k DAU on a chat feature, no overage at typical use
  • 5 namespaces: prod / staging / dev / two side projects
  • Compile + distill + quantize: turn captures into a signed .kolm
  • K-score + frozen eval: "the model is still good" is a number, pinned over time
  • Private registry: your artifacts stay in your tenant
  • Drift detection: gateway noticed a 2.3pt K drop since last build, alerts you
  • Verify-anywhere: anyone with your public key can confirm the .kolm is exactly what shipped
  • Email support, 24h response: an actual engineer, not a chatbot

FAQ

Common questions about billing.

What counts as a "gateway call"?
Every request that hits api.kolm.ai/v1/* and gets routed to a model. Verify, capture-replay, registry queries, doctor checks, and dashboard activity do not count.
Do I keep paying after I compile?
No. Once a .kolm exists and you're running it on your own hardware (laptop, server, phone, browser, VPC, air-gap), there's no per-call charge. You only pay your kolm plan + your own hardware + your own electricity. The gateway plan covers ongoing capture, drift detection, and registry hosting.
What if I go over the call limit?
Overage at $0.50 / 1,000 calls, billed on the next invoice. We email you at 50%, 80%, and 100% so there are no surprises. You can also set a hard cap that returns HTTP 429 to your code: useful if you want to enforce a budget at the infra layer.
Can I self-host the whole stack?
Yes, on every plan, including Free. Apache-2.0 source, Docker Compose, Kubernetes Helm chart, plus an air-gap bundle on Team and Business. The hosted gateway is a convenience: the open-source repo is the real product. See /self-host.
Is there a startup or nonprofit discount?
Yes. 75% off Pro for funded startups under $5M ARR (for 12 months, renewable). Free Team for registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits. Free Business for accredited universities and open-research labs. Email hello@kolm.ai with proof and we usually turn it around in 24h.
Do you offer annual pricing?
Yes, ~17% off (you pay 10 months for 12) on Pro and Team via invoice. Business is annual by default. Email hello@kolm.ai or use /enterprise for an MSA + annual PO.
Where does my data live? Can it be moved?
By default, in our US-East tenant (AWS us-east-1, single region, fully encrypted at rest with our KMS keys). EU residency (eu-west-1) and APAC (ap-southeast-1) on Business. Captures and artifacts are tenant-fenced: no other tenant can read them. You can export everything at any time via /v1/registry/export and /v1/captures/export. See /security.
Can I bring my own API keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)?
Yes, on every plan. The gateway can route to your provider keys instead of its own pool. Useful when you have provider credits to burn, when you want consolidated billing on one provider, or when your provider contract requires you to use your own key. The gateway-fee still applies, but the inference cost lands on your provider invoice.
Are there usage-based tiers I'm missing?
No. The four prices on this page are the four prices. If you need more than 50M calls/month, custom data residency, multi-region BYOC, or a co-located deploy, that's an MSA conversation with enterprise. We won't invent a fifth tier just to dance around a number.
How does refund work?
30 days, no questions asked, on the first invoice on any plan. After that, prorated refund on cancellation. No "early termination" fee, no minimum commitment on Pro / Team. Business annual is refundable in proportion to the unused portion of the commitment.
Do you sell my data, train on my captures, or share with third parties?
No, no, no. Captures are tenant-fenced and only used to compile artifacts that you own. We don't train any shared model on customer data. Our subprocessors are listed at /subprocessors: they're infrastructure (AWS, Vercel, Stripe) not data buyers.
Can I get a custom contract / MSA / DPA?
Yes on Business. We use a short, English-language MSA and standard SCC-based DPA. Most procurement reviews close in under 3 weeks. Email hello@kolm.ai with your standard paper and we'll redline.
What happens if I cancel?
Your gateway access stops at the end of the billing period. Your published .kolm artifacts keep running on your own hardware (we have no way to disable them: that's the whole point). You can download all captures + artifacts before cancelling. After 90 days post-cancellation, captures are purged from our side; published artifacts persist on whatever hardware you put them.
Is there a per-seat charge on Team / Business?
No. Team and Business are flat-rate. Add as many seats as your org has: SCIM will auto-provision on Business. We charge for the platform, not for the people using it.
What's NOT included on Business that I might expect?
Custom model training (we distill from your captures, we don't pre-train), 24/7 phone support (we do 24/7 Slack on Business + named CSM, not phone), and on-premises deploys outside of BYOC (Business runs in your cloud account, not on your bare-metal data-center hardware: that's a separate conversation).
