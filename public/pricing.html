06FAQ

Questions buyers ask.

Not here? GitHub Discussions or dev@kolm.ai.

Do I need to understand fine-tuning or distillation?

No. Give examples and a task description. kolm handles the rest, including all 4 recipe classes (rule, synthesized_rule, compiled_rule, distilled_model) across the full base-model range from 0.5B (laptop scale) to 1.6T (frontier scale).

Who pays for the model API calls?

You do. kolm uses your key only at compile. Never resells tokens, never marks up inference.

What counts as a compile?

One billed compile = one job that reaches the K-score gate (pass or fail). Re-runs from the same spec inside the same calendar day don't double-bill on Pro and Enterprise. Sub-gate failures are free.

What does one compile produce?

A signed recipe-mode artifact with eval and receipt evidence. Specialized adapters, recall indexes, and model rebuilds are bridge targets.

What if K-score is below the gate?

Artifact doesn't ship. You see the breakdown, adjust, recompile. No charge for sub-gate compiles on Pro and Enterprise.

How big is an artifact?

Recipe-mode (v0.2 ships today): small signed zip. Model-backed (runtime-bridge engaged): 20-200 MB for a 1B-7B INT4 base + adapter. Recall index shards above 4 GB.

Can I run an artifact offline?

Recipe-mode runs through the CLI. Detached model execution requires the runtime bridge.

Which frontier providers work as teachers?

Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, Together, Fireworks, Bedrock, Azure, or your gateway. Any OpenAI or Messages-contract endpoint.

Which base models can I target?

Qwen 2.5/3.x, Llama 3.x, Phi-3, Hermes 3, Gemma 2 at INT4, 1B-14B. Enterprise supports private bases.

Free trial?

14 days of Pro, cancel any time. Trial artifacts stay yours, runnable forever.

Do Teams really start at two seats?

Yes. Teams is $149/mo flat for any team between 2 and 10 seats. Past 10, it's +$30/extra seat. No five-seat minimum, no pretending two-person teams are an edge case.

Is annual billing cheaper?

Yes. Annual is two months free on every paid tier (about 17% off). Switch any time from /account. Annual is the default toggle.

Can I export everything?

Artifact is a signed zip you own. Recall index exports as Parquet, receipts as JSONL. Nothing locked to our cloud.

Non-profit or academic pricing?

Pro is free for verified non-profits and accredited researchers. Mail us with a .edu or .org address.
