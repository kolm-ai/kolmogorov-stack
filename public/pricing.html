FAQ · WHAT YOU PAY FOR

What your money buys.

One plan takes you the whole way — from your live API calls to a portable, signed model you run on your own hardware. Here's how the pieces price out.

FAQ · ESTIMATE

Which plan is right for me?

Tell us your call volume, compile work, seats, SSO, and where you'll deploy, and GET /v1/pricing/estimate points you at a plan — the same plans you see here, so the estimate matches your signup.

FAQ · CREDITS

Why are compile credits their own line?

Because building a model is the part that creates something you own — so you can see exactly what you pay for it, never buried in infrastructure. One credit builds one model: examples, recipe, evals, manifest, and signed receipt.

FAQ · CONTROLS

What comes with the bigger plans?

Seats, role-based access, shared workspaces, provider policy, and exports — real controls wired into the product, not generic add-ons stapled onto a seat count.

FAQ · PORTABILITY

Can I take my work with me?

Yes — it's the whole point. Your models, manifests, receipts, reports, and procurement packets all export and work outside our UI. No lock-in. Even the plan data is open at /v1/plans and /v1/billing/tiers.
