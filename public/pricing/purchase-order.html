Purchase orders and enterprise terms.

Not Kolm therapeutics, Kolm band, or other unrelated organizations using similar names.

Procurement-friendly billing for Business and Enterprise tiers. Net-30 by default, Net-60 on request. We accept your standard PO format, invoice once per period, and never require a credit card on file.

rodneyyesep@gmail.com -> See standard pricing

Available tiers

Purchase orders are available on the Business and Enterprise tiers. Pro is self-serve credit-card-only because the contract value does not justify the procurement overhead -- if you need a PO at Pro volume, talk to us and we will quote a Business plan.

Business Mid-market and growth-stage teams Annual commitment. PO accepted. MSA on request. No architecture review included; engineering Slack channel available on the upgrade SKU. Standard SLA: 99.5% gateway uptime; 4-hour business-hours support response.
Enterprise Regulated, air-gapped, BYOC, procurement-led Custom annual contract. PO accepted. MSA, BAA, DPA on request. Architecture review included. Standard SLA: 99.9% gateway uptime; named CSM; private Slack channel; 1-hour business-hours response. See enterprise for the full surface.

Payment terms

  • Net-30 default. Invoice issued at contract start; payment due within 30 days. No early-payment discount; no late-payment penalty for the first 15 days past due.
  • Net-60 on request. Available for any tier on request, no markup. Common for government and large-enterprise procurement cycles.
  • Annual prepay. 8% discount on the contract value when paid annually in advance. Settles to a single invoice on the contract start date.
  • Quarterly billing. Available for multi-year contracts with a single quarterly invoice.
  • Currency. USD default. Other currencies on the currency localization page.
  • Wire transfer. Accepted for all PO-billed contracts; account details on the invoice.

Process

End-to-end timeline is typically 2 to 4 weeks from first email to deployed artifact, faster if your procurement team can move on Net-30 and the MSA template already aligns.

  1. Request quote. Email rodneyyesep@gmail.com with the fields in the required-info checklist below. We respond within one business day with a written quote pinned to a six-month price lock.
  2. PO submission. Your procurement team issues a PO referencing the quote number. We accept PDF or your standard procurement-portal format. No special PO template required.
  3. Invoice. We issue an invoice tied to the PO within one business day of receipt. Invoice references both the PO number and the kolm quote ID.
  4. Deploy. Account provisioning starts immediately on invoice acknowledgement -- you do not have to wait for payment to clear. SSO, tenant setup, and first artifact compile can run in parallel with accounts-payable processing.
  5. Payment. Wire transfer or ACH per the terms on the invoice. Payment clears, contract anchors to the start date on the quote, and the renewal clock starts.

Required info checklist

Send the items below in your initial email and we can get a quote out the same day.

Procurement starter checklist

  • Legal company name as it appears on invoices.
  • Billing address.
  • Accounts-payable contact name and email.
  • Tax ID (EIN, VAT, GST, etc.) for tax-exempt or international invoicing.
  • Preferred tier (Business or Enterprise) and headline volume estimate (gateway calls / compiles / namespaces).
  • Preferred contract term (1 year, 2 years, 3 years).
  • Preferred billing cadence (annual prepay, quarterly, monthly).
  • Preferred payment terms (Net-30, Net-60).
  • Currency.
  • Any required MSA, DPA, BAA, or vendor-security-review templates you would like reviewed before quote.
  • Target deployment date.

Honesty contract. The quote is fixed for six months. If our list price drops in that window, your quote drops with it -- you never pay more than the current list. If our list price rises, your quote is locked at the original number for the duration of the contract.

What is NOT covered by a PO. Overage on gateway calls or compile credits beyond the contract volume is billed monthly in arrears against the same PO, not auto-debited. We will email at 50%, 80%, and 100% of the contract cap so accounts payable can plan an amendment if usage is growing.
