Student pricing.

Free kolm Pro for active students. The whole tier -- compile, distill, quantize, K-score, signed registry, teacher credits -- on us. The only thing we ask is that you re-verify once a year so the entitlement stays attached to a real, currently-enrolled student.

dev@kolm.ai -> See standard pricing

Eligibility

  • Active enrollment at an accredited college, university, or equivalent institution.
  • An institutional email -- a .edu address in the US, or the local equivalent (e.g., .ac.uk, .edu.in, university-issued mailbox on a national TLD).
  • Or, if your school does not issue institutional email, a current student-ID scan plus a brief note from a faculty advisor.
  • Open to undergraduate, graduate, and postdoc students. Bootcamps are evaluated case by case -- write in.

What you get

The full free-Pro entitlement -- not a stripped-down student edition. You ship the same artifacts a paying customer ships.

  • 500,000 gateway calls / month.
  • 5 namespaces, captures with rolling retention.
  • Compile, distill, quantize, K-score.
  • Frozen evals and drift detection.
  • Private registry + signed artifacts.
  • Verify-anywhere with public key.
  • Teacher credits -- enough monthly credit on the kolm-hosted Anthropic and OpenAI teacher route to compile a small artifact end-to-end without bringing your own provider key. (Bring your own key any time -- the credit is a starter ramp, not a quota cap.)
  • Email support, 24h response, same queue as paying Pro customers.

How to apply

Email the address below with the fields below. The whole form fits in one message; we usually approve within two business days.

Student application -- send via email

Name
Your full name as it appears on the student ID.
School
Institution name and country.
Email
Institutional address (or personal address plus ID scan).
Advisor
Name and email of a current faculty advisor or instructor we can ping if anything is unclear.
What you are building
One or two sentences -- the artifact, the corpus, the runtime target. Coursework counts; research projects count; side projects count.
Email dev@kolm.ai to apply -> See docs

Renewal

Yearly re-verification. Two weeks before the anniversary, we email you a one-question form: "are you still actively enrolled?" One reply, one check, one renewal. We never auto-bill a former student.

  • If your status changes mid-year (graduation, dropout, leave of absence), let us know -- the entitlement converts to a free-tier account, no penalty, no surprise charge.
  • If you forget to renew, the account drops to free tier at the anniversary date; nothing is deleted; you can re-apply any time.
  • Graduating into a job? Talk to your employer about paid Pro or Team. We are happy to migrate the artifacts you already built.

Plain constraint

Student credits do not roll over month-to-month. The teacher-credit allotment resets on the first of each month. Unused credit does not accumulate. We do this because the budget is sized to a typical student usage pattern, and unbounded rollover lets a single student stockpile credit for a year and then burn it in a weekend -- which is unfair to the next student. Real working budget; just no banking.
