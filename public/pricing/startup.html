Startup program.

Free kolm Pro for 12 months for portfolio companies of the accelerators below. The whole stack -- gateway, compile credits, signed registry, K-score, drift detection -- so the first artifact ships before the runway debate even starts.

dev@kolm.ai -> See standard pricing

Eligibility

Active or recent (within the last 24 months) portfolio company of any of:

Y CombinatorAny batch, including continuity follow-ons.
TechstarsAny program, any city.
500 Startups500 Global accelerator and pre-accelerator cohorts.
AntlerFounder-stage and growth-stage portfolios.
Sequoia ArcThe Arc cohort program specifically.
Equivalent programsTop-tier accelerators not listed -- write in. We approve most.

What you get

Free kolm Pro for 12 months from the activation date. Same Pro tier paying customers use -- not a stripped-down "startup edition."

  • 500,000 gateway calls / month.
  • 5 namespaces with rolling capture retention.
  • Compile, distill, quantize, K-score.
  • Frozen evals and drift detection.
  • Private registry + signed artifacts.
  • Verify-anywhere with public key.
  • Email support, 24h response.
  • Direct line to engineering for architecture review on your first artifact.

If you outgrow Pro mid-program (real volume, real users), we will upgrade you to Team gratis for the remainder of the 12 months and write up the case study together.

How to apply

One email. We need proof of program membership -- the simplest forms are listed below, in order of how fast we can approve.

  • LP email. Forward (or CC) the program partner who introduced you to the cohort. We just need one line from them confirming you are in their portfolio.
  • Batch letter. A scan of the acceptance letter / contract / SAFE that names the program -- enough to verify the affiliation.
  • Public portfolio listing. If you are listed on the program's public companies page, send the link.
  • Founder-program-issued perks portal. If you have an existing perks-portal entry for kolm.ai, the deal code is the proof.
Email dev@kolm.ai to apply -> See docs

After 12 months

The program does not auto-renew, and it does not silently flip you onto a paid invoice. Two weeks before the anniversary you get an email with two boxes to tick.

Two paths at month 12.

  • Auto-convert to Pro paid. The same tier you have been on, at standard $49 / month. Most teams pick this -- the artifacts you built during the program stay live, no migration, no downtime.
  • Drop to Free with notice. Account converts to the free tier; gateway cap drops to 50,000 calls / month; compile is paused until you upgrade or renew. Your artifacts keep running on your own hardware regardless -- the runtime is Apache-2.0 and never phones home.

No silent invoices. If you do not reply to the anniversary email, we default to "drop to Free with notice" -- never auto-charge a card. The Pro tier resumes only when you explicitly opt in.
