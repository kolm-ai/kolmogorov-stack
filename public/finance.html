How customer data stays inside

What makes this examinable.

No vendor-speak. The exact mechanism for each property, with the CLI verb you can verify yourself the first time you install kolm.

01

The data plane runs in your VPC

On-prem or BYOC. Customer records are processed by code running in your AWS / GCP / Azure account or your data center. No outbound calls to kolm.ai except optional metering, which can be turned off and replaced with a local meter.

02

Compile is residency-pinned

Compile region selectable EU, UK, US, APAC. The frontier teacher reaches your training set through your existing egress proxy. The compile pipeline never persists customer data.

03

Encryption at rest and in transit

TLS 1.3 in transit. AES-256-GCM at rest with a per-tenant key in your KMS (AWS KMS, GCP KMS, Azure Key Vault). Keys rotate quarterly by default; you can force rotation any time.

04

MNPI redaction at the boundary

kolm capture log --redact mnpi strips counterparty names, position sizes, and tier-2 material non-public terms using Presidio plus a finance-NER overlay before the capture is persisted. The redactor is a worker pod; you can swap it for your own without touching the rest of the system.

05

Audit trail to your SIEM

Every read, write, model invocation, and capture is in the audit log with desk, route, IP, and the receipt CID. Streams to Splunk, Datadog, or Elastic in JSON or CEF. Append-only with a signed chain, FINRA 4511-compatible.

06

Signed artifact + signed inference

The .kolm artifact is signed with your tenant's Ed25519 key. Each inference call returns a per-call receipt linking the input hash, the output hash, and the artifact CID. That receipt is your chain of custody if a trade is ever subject to FINRA exam or SEC enforcement.

07

Model is pinned for the life of the deployment

Frontier model deprecations affect future compiles, not in-flight artifacts. Quarterly MRM review on every frontier upgrade is no longer required; the artifact is the model. The firm decides when to recompile and re-attest.

Honest scope

What kolm does, and what we tell every finance buyer up front.

No alpha claims, no auto-trade language. Read this before the rest of the page becomes interesting.

A

Kolm is not an investment-decision system

The CLI does not output trades. Our distilled artifacts route, summarize, structure, draft, redact, and triage. Anything that crosses into an investment decision or order routing is your application logic, your label, and your supervisory path.

B

We do not file your MRM dossier

If you are deploying a kolm artifact in production, your model-risk team owns the SR 11-7 sign-off. What kolm gives you: the manifest fields and signed receipts your committee needs to actually review the model, not a vendor PowerPoint claiming it is safe.

C

Models will be wrong sometimes

Our distilled models drop K-score by under 3 points after quantization. They still hallucinate, miss edge cases, and exhibit bias. Every kolm workflow ships with a defer-on-low-confidence path and a human-in-the-loop gate by default; turning that off is a choice you have to make on purpose, with your supervisor's name on it.

D

We do not sign a vendor agreement that lets you skip MRM

If you are using kolm for anything that touches a regulated process, we will ask whether your MRM has reviewed the artifact. Period. Even for a pilot. We will help you build the dossier; we will not sign a contract that papers over its absence.

Reference architectures

How finance teams actually deploy kolm.

Same product, several placements. Pick the one that matches your IT estate and your compliance posture.

A. Desk-side

On the trader's or analyst's laptop

Single signed .kolm file deploys via MDM. The artifact runs locally with zero network calls. P95 latency 80 ms on M3 Pro for a 7B INT4 specialist. Audit log syncs when the laptop next reaches the network.

$ kolm run credit-memo.kolm \
--in borrower-file.json \
--out memo-draft.md \
--offline
B. BYOC region-pinned

Inside the bank's cloud account

Helm chart installed in the same AWS / GCP / Azure account that runs your post-trade systems. Residency pinned to your selected region. PHI flows through your VPC peering; kolm never sees an external network.

helm install kolm kolm/finance \
--set residency=eu-west-1 \
--set kms.arn=arn:aws:kms:... \
--set egress=none
C. On-prem with DMZ

Bare metal, audit-only egress

kolm runs in your data center on Kubernetes or bare metal. The only outbound path is the audit-log stream to your SIEM (Splunk, Elastic, Sentinel). No vendor cloud is in the path. FFIEC vendor-management posture is "tool" not "service".

$ kolm install --on-prem \
--bundle kolm-2026-05.tar.zst \
--signature kolm-2026-05.sig \
--egress audit-only
D. Air-gap appliance

Signed OVA in a regulated enclave

Signed OVA appliance that boots offline in a research or trading enclave. Pre-loaded model registry. Upgrades arrive on a signed USB or a one-way data diode; the appliance verifies signatures before applying.

appliance: kolm-2026-05.ova
signature: kolm-2026-05.sig
verify: gpg --verify (offline)
boot: zero outbound

Operational footprint

What the operations team needs to know.

Day-2 facts. Capacity, paging, change management, the boring numbers that decide whether this is something you can actually run.

i

Footprint per desk or trading floor

Reference deployment: three application nodes (8 vCPU, 32 GB RAM each), one inference node with a single A10G or RTX 4000 Ada (24 GB VRAM), one Postgres instance, one S3-compatible bucket. Handles ~3,000 inferences per hour on a 7B INT4 specialist with headroom for a 150-trader desk.

ii

Change management

Upgrades arrive as signed bundles every two weeks (security) or monthly (features). The bundle includes a release note, a signature, and a rollback receipt. You apply on your cadence; we do not push to your cluster. Compatible with your existing CAB.

iii

Paging

Optional vendor-side paging. For BYOC and on-prem your own oncall pages first; we are available 24/7 under enterprise contracts with a 1-hour response on Sev-1 and a 4-hour response on Sev-2.

iv

Disaster recovery

RPO 15 min, RTO 4 hours on hosted. BYOC and on-prem inherit your DR posture; we ship runbooks for restoring captures, registry artifacts, and signing keys from cold storage. Tabletop exercises are part of the onboarding kit.

v

Telemetry

Prometheus-format metrics on /metrics (opt-in). OpenTelemetry traces (opt-in). Structured logs on stdout. Zero customer-record content in any telemetry stream by default; opt-in flags add only structural identifiers (artifact CID, namespace name).

vi

Cost

For BYOC the cost is your cloud bill plus our flat enterprise license. For SaaS-hosted, the public Enterprise tier starts at $4,000/mo for 5M gateway calls. Talk to us if you have more than 50M/month; we have not blown anyone's budget yet.
