01 · Distill

Compile a workload-specific model from your traffic.

Teacher and student both run in your perimeter. Customer data and trade-flow never leave. The captured patterns become the dataset; the artifact is the deliverable.

$ kolm distill --namespace research-summary --base qwen2.5-7b

Compliance posture

Frameworks your model-risk team already lives with.

Kolm does not write your model risk document for you. It produces the evidence your validators need and the controls your auditors expect.

Where finance teams ship kolm

Workloads we see in production.

Every workflow ships with a defer-on-low-confidence path and a human gate by default; turning either off is an explicit switch.

Research desk

Earnings-call summarizer

Reads the transcript + the model file, returns a structured summary against your firm's research template, cites the line in the transcript per claim.

input: transcript
output: research-brief + cites
defer: low-confidence
Compliance

Surveillance triage

Reads chat + voice transcripts, scores against your lexicon and behavioural model, surfaces the candidate alerts with the cite trail attached.

input: comms + lexicon
output: ranked-alerts + cites
review: human-confirmed
Operations

Trade-exception triage

Reads the breaks queue, classifies the exception, drafts the resolution note. Anything off-pattern defers to a human; nothing posts without an ops sign-off.

input: exception-record
output: class + draft-note
off-pattern: defer
Credit + underwriting

Document extraction

Pulls covenants, schedules, and financials out of credit agreements and term sheets, normalizes against your data model, leaves the credit call to the analyst.

input: agreement-pdf
output: structured-fields + spans
analyst-review: required
Wealth + client

Client-comms drafting

Drafts the client letter against your house style, attaches the disclosure block your supervisory desk requires. Send is reviewer-approved.

input: account + house-style
output: draft + disclosures
send: reviewer-approved
Risk

Policy-bounded Q&A

Reads your internal policy library, answers in-band only, defers to the policy desk on anything beyond the cite-able universe. Receipts log the policy spans used per answer.

input: question + policy-corpus
output: answer + policy-cites
out-of-policy: defer
