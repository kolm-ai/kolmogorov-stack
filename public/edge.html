Why kolm

Why kolm for edge

Q4_K_M / IQ4_XS GGUF

Sub-1 GB for 7B models, sub-500 MB for 3B. The compile step picks the quant ladder rung that fits the hardware target you declare.

Single-binary runtime

No Python, no Docker, no GPU driver dance. Statically linked llama.cpp with the artifact embedded or sideloaded next to the executable.

Offline-first

Distill happens on your dev box. The deployed binary needs no network — not for licensing, not for telemetry, not for inference.

Signed receipts

Even on edge, every inference signs Ed25519 to a local journal. Sync the journal back upstream when the device next sees a network, or never.

What we do not hide

Constraints we do not hide

Constraints:

An edge-quantized 7B will not beat GPT-4 on every task. The eval harness shows you which workflows are safe to run at the edge before you ship — per task, per device, against the teacher you distilled from. Ship the ones that pass. Route the ones that fail back to the gateway.
