How it works

Capture. Distill. Quantize. Run.

Use them as a pipeline to turn production traffic into a private model. Or use any one alone.

01 · Route

One drop-in URL.

export OPENAI_BASE_URL=api.kolm.ai/v1

Every OpenAI, Anthropic, vLLM, or SGLang client now routes through your gateway. Failover and PII redaction at the edge.

02 · Capture

Approved calls become a dataset.

kolm capture · auto

Each approved response is signed and appended to a tenant-isolated lake. Hash-chained, replayable, exportable as JSONL / Parquet / HF.

03 · Distill + quant

Teach a smaller model, compress it.

kolm distill --namespace ns

Pick one of 5 distillation strategies and any of 10 quantization methods. K-Score gates every build before it ships.

04 · Deploy

One signed file, your hardware.

kolm run ns.kolm

vLLM, SGLang, TensorRT-LLM, llama.cpp, MLX, ONNX, Core ML, WebGPU. Every response signed Ed25519, verifiable offline.

Point your OpenAI client at api.kolm.ai/v1 and ship as you always do; every approved response is hashed, signed, and logged inside your tenant. When the namespace has enough rows, kolm distill teaches a smaller open student from your captures and bake-off-scores it against the teacher before it ships. kolm quantize shrinks the result 4× at INT4 with a K‑Score gate. kolm run picks the backend for whatever you're targeting — vLLM, llama.cpp, MLX, WebGPU — and signs every response with a key your auditor can verify offline.

FAQ

Five questions, no marketing.

The other five — drift, fine-tune vs distill, verify-only, export, cancel — are at /faq.

01 Is kolm a replacement for OpenAI? +
No. kolm sits in front of OpenAI (and Anthropic, vLLM, SGLang, TRT-LLM). You keep the SDK you already use. We route, log approvals, and let you compile those approved calls into a smaller model you own. After compile, you stop paying per token.
02 What is a .kolm artifact? +
One sealed file that contains the model weights, the spec it was compiled against, the seed split it trained on, the teacher receipts, and an Ed25519 signature binding them all. Verify any response a .kolm produces at /verify-prod.
03 Where does the data live? Can I self-host? +
Every capture, dataset, and artifact is tenant-isolated and tenant-fenced. Your data never crosses to another tenant. The whole stack runs on your own hardware (BYOC, VPC, on-prem, air-gapped) under Apache-2.0. See /self-host.
04 How real are the benchmarks on this page? +
Run them yourself. The 32B-class → 17.9 GB INT4 in 125 s number was measured on an RTX 5090 with bitsandbytes 0.49.2 (NF4 + double quant) and torch 2.11.0+cu128. Model identities and transcripts are at /benchmarks.
05 What about HIPAA, SOC 2, regulated workloads? +
SOC 2 Type I available today. HIPAA mapping (/hipaa-mapping) and a signed BAA (/baa) are Enterprise controls. Explicit doors for healthcare, finance, legal, defense, and EU/sovereign deployments. PII redaction (text, image, audio) on by default at the gateway.
