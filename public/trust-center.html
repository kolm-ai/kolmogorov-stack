02 / Mechanics

What a Trust Link is, and what we count.

Capability token

Possession is the grant

Each link carries an unguessable slug. Anyone holding it can open the signed report; nobody can enumerate it. Revoke the link and the grant is gone.

Engagement

Views and distinct viewers

Every open is counted, and repeat opens by the same viewer collapse into one distinct viewer. A reviewer who keeps coming back is a deal that is moving.

Privacy

Hashed at the boundary

Viewer addresses are hashed before they are stored, so engagement is visible to you while the viewer's identity is never kept in the clear.
