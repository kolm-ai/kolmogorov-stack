Publish

Publish your recipe in one command.

The CLI builds a signed recipe bundle, pushes the receipt to the registry, and (if you opted in) lists the artifact on the marketplace. Weights stay where you put them.

# Publish a recipe with a license and a price. $ kolm recipe publish my-sota-7b --license=apache-2.0 --price=free [pack] bundle recipe + eval pack + receipt chain [sign] Ed25519 over JCS-canonical receipt [push] registry POST /v1/recipes · cid bafkrei...4f7c [list] marketplace entry created · status: public [link] kolm.ai/r/my-sota-7b

Free + open

Apache-2.0, MIT, BSD. Anyone can fork and re-quantize. Your receipt stays in the parent chain of every derivative.

Pay-what-you-want

Suggested tip; users decide. Stripe-backed. You keep 80%. The registry records every paid pull with a signed receipt.

Premium licensed

Set a per-seat or per-inference fee. Custom EULA. You keep 70%. Enterprise buyers can ship the receipt through their procurement chain.
