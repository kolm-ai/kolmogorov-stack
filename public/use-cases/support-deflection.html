Customer support deflection

How does kolm handle customer support deflection?

kolm captures your real support conversations, distills them into a small specialist that asks the right clarifying question first, and quantizes it to your own hardware — so support answers run at fixed cost with a signed build receipt instead of a per-ticket token bill. The result is a model you own and run on your own hardware, trained on your real workload, with a signed build receipt instead of a recurring per-call API bill.

The problem

What makes customer support deflection expensive today?

High-volume support teams pay per token to a frontier model for answers that follow a narrow, repetitive playbook. The bill scales with ticket volume and the model is generic, not tuned to your product or tone. That cost compounds with volume and ties a sensitive workload to an external API, which is exactly the situation an owned, compiled specialist is designed to replace once the traffic pattern is well understood.

The kolm flow

How does the kolm pipeline solve it?

Point your support assistant at the kolm gateway. It captures (question, resolution) pairs with PII redacted on the way in. Once the lake is full, kolm distills a 7B specialist, gates it on the K-score, and compiles a quantized artifact you serve yourself. Each stage runs inside your own environment, so capture, distillation, and compilation happen without handing the workload to a third party, and the final artifact is a portable, signed model.

The outcome

What do you end up with?

The result is a support model you own: it follows your playbook, runs on hardware you control, and every build is signed and replayable — eliminating the per-token bill that scaled with ticket volume. Because the artifact is portable and signed, you can move it between a laptop, a VPC, or an air-gapped network, and prove at any point exactly which weights and method produced it.

FAQ

Will the specialist hallucinate fewer details?
In the published benchmark the distilled specialist invented no fabricated details (100% no-inventions) and asked for missing information rather than guessing.
How much support traffic do I need?
Distillation works from a few hundred high-quality captured pairs; the trinity-500 benchmark used a council-distilled set evaluated on 57 held-out cases.
Can it run on-prem?
Yes. The compiled specialist is a portable .kolm artifact that runs in your VPC or air-gapped, with no per-token bill.
