kolm.ai · service-level agreement

99.95% monthly uptime for the hosted plane.

If we miss it, you get service credits. Your compiled artifacts run on your hardware, so an SLA breach never blocks inference — only the hosted control plane and managed compile/distill jobs.

What is covered

  • The hosted API at https://kolm.ai/v1/* (capture, distill, compile, replay, registry, signin).
  • The hosted dashboard at https://kolm.ai and account surfaces.
  • Managed compile and distill workers when the cloud target is selected.

What is not covered

  • Self-hosted runtime: by design, your .kolm artifact has no runtime dependency on our cloud.
  • Third-party model providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) when you proxy through us. Their SLAs apply.
  • Scheduled maintenance windows announced at least 48 hours in advance.

How to file a claim

  1. Within 30 days of the incident, email sla@kolm.ai.
  2. Include your workspace ID, the time window, and any affected request IDs.
  3. We confirm against the 90-day public history on /status. Credits post to the next invoice.
