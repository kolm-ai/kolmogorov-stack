Posture

Confidentiality and ethical-rule alignment.

Kolm is the architecture under your existing duty of confidentiality and competence. It does not give legal advice; it gives the desk that does the tools to do it without sending client content to a third-party endpoint.

Privilege · ABA 1.6 · 5.3
No third-party inference path by default

Inference runs in your network. Egress is audit-only. The vendor (kolm) never sees client work product at inference time. Security details

Conflicts + matter walls
Per-namespace isolation

One namespace per matter or per ethical wall. Cross-namespace replay fails signature verification. Wall enforcement is mechanical, not policy-only.

e-discovery
FRCP-friendly chain

Per-call receipts plus an append-only audit log streamed to your archive. Output can be exported into a Relativity-friendly load file.

Where legal teams ship kolm

Patterns from production.

Every workflow ships with a defer-on-low-confidence path and an attorney sign-off gate. Turning either off is a deliberate switch.

Transactional

Contract review against firm playbook

Reads inbound contracts, marks the redline list against your firm playbook, drafts the negotiation note. Associate reviews + sends.

input: contract + playbook
output: redline + note
send: attorney-approved
Litigation

Document review triage

Pre-tags the review queue by relevance, privilege, and key issue, leaves a confident audit trail of the spans that drove each decision.

input: doc + issue-tags
output: priority + cited-spans
defer: low-confidence
Diligence

Data-room synthesis

Reads the data-room index, drafts the diligence memo against your firm's structure, attaches the source-doc cite per finding.

input: data-room-index
output: diligence-memo + cites
cite-required: yes
In-house

Policy-bounded Q&A

Reads your internal playbooks, answers in-band only, defers to the legal desk on anything outside the cite-able universe.

input: question + policy
output: answer + cite
off-policy: defer
Knowledge

Matter-pattern retrieval

Surfaces analogous prior matters by issue, jurisdiction, and outcome. Hand-off to a senior associate is the default action.

input: issue + jurisdiction
output: analog-matters + reasoning
action: senior-handoff
Court & filings

Brief skeleton drafting

Drafts the brief skeleton against your jurisdiction's local rules, attaches the citator status per cite, leaves substantive argument to the partner.

input: matter + local-rules
output: brief-skeleton + cites
cite-validation: shepardize
