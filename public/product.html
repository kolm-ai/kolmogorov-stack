kolm.ai / Product

The AI compiler for local AI.

Capture your real prompts. Compile them into your own model. Ship it on your hardware. Audit every call. kolm.ai starts as an OpenAI-compatible gateway, then turns reviewed traffic into signed artifacts that run locally.

Start as a gateway. Become a compiler.

The first value is immediate: observe cost, latency, privacy, repeats, and provider performance. The compounding value is what the lake lets you build later.

# Drop-in capture
export OPENAI_BASE_URL=http://127.0.0.1:8787/v1
export OPENAI_API_KEY=kolm_local_dev

# Your app keeps using its normal client.
# kolm stores every approved row locally.
# Promote the repeat
kolm datasets build support-triage --from lake
kolm train support-triage --teacher openrouter:auto
kolm compile support-triage --target gemma-3n
kolm verify support-triage.kolm

Use the models. Keep the data advantage.

kolm.ai lets builders learn from their own traffic instead of donating every workflow insight to a frontier provider forever.
