The product

One platform for your whole team's AI.

kolm is four jobs in one control plane: connect every tool and see every call, build models you own from your own data, run them anywhere from the cloud to your own machines, and govern the whole thing with a signed record. Every path converges on one place you control.

Connect → Own → Run anywhere → Govern

01 What it does

Connect

See and control every call.

Point any OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or vLLM client at kolm. It routes every request, attributes it to the person and team who made it, redacts PII inline, and stores it. You get cost per team and per model, repeated-prompt clusters, and access control with SSO, SAML, and SCIM.

7 protocols · one endpoint · live team dashboard

Own

Build models you own from your own work.

kolm finds the work your team repeats and turns it into a dataset. A light run distills and quantizes a small specialist in minutes; an intense run distills from a council of frontier teachers and fine-tunes on rented cloud GPUs. You keep the weights, the data, and the receipts.

browser builder or CLI · light or cloud-GPU training · one .kolm file

Run anywhere

Run on your own machines, not just someone else's cloud.

Shrink a capable model to run on hardware you already own: a 7B model on a workstation, a 32B model on a single high-end GPU. Export to GGUF, Ollama, or Hugging Face, reach it over a local endpoint or SSH, or keep using hosted models. You choose per model and per team.

local · VPC · air-gapped · DeepSeek-R1-32B at 17.9 GB INT4 on one RTX 5090

Govern

Govern it, and prove the controls held.

SSO, SCIM, and RBAC are the floor. BYOC keeps training and inference inside your boundary, residency spans nine regions, and a signed Ed25519 receipt on every call makes every control checkable after the fact, by you or an auditor, offline.

SSO/SCIM/RBAC · BYOC · 9 regions · signed receipts · SOC 2 Type I

It is one product, not four tools.

Capture feeds what you own, what you own runs anywhere, and governance wraps all of it. Start with visibility today and grow into models your company owns.
