Compliance posture

Built for student data.

What the architecture gives you. What still belongs to your CIO and registrar.

FERPA
20 USC 1232g · 34 CFR 99

No outbound network. Education records stay inside your institutional boundary. We are not a school official unless your DPA names us.

COPPA
K-12 deployments

No third-party data sharing in the runtime path. Operator obligations stay with the district. Receipts simplify §312.4 disclosure.

GDPR (EU institutions)

Self-hosted = institution remains the controller. Receipts give you Art. 30 records of processing for free.

Campus workflows

Six patterns in classrooms now.

What instructors, TAs, and research labs are shipping.

Instructor

Office-hours tutor

Answers from the course textbook and lecture notes. Refuses out-of-syllabus prompts. Cites the page or slide used.

in: student question · out: answer + lecture citation
TA

Rubric-aligned feedback

Grade-adjacent feedback against the published rubric. The TA stays the grader of record; the model drafts the comment.

in: student essay · out: rubric-keyed comments
Research lab

Literature triage

Cluster 2k papers against your reading list. Surface methods, datasets, and metrics. Replayable across the cohort.

in: arXiv batch · out: ranked papers + method table
Registrar

Policy & transcript Q&A

Answer student questions against the catalog and academic-policy library. Cites the rule paragraph used.

in: question · out: answer + catalog page #
Library

Research-help desk

Direct students to the right databases and call numbers. No external lookups; uses your discovery-service index.

in: topic · out: ranked sources + call numbers
K-12 district

IEP draft assistant

Draft IEP goals against your district template. The case manager owns the final document; the model drafts the language.

in: goal target + history · out: drafted IEP section
