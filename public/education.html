01Regulatory framing

Controls your CIO and IRB already read.

Each control below is described as the kolm posture today. We name the Wave marker for every control so your procurement memo can cite the artifact.

FERPA
redactor + boundary

Student records (PII, grades, disciplinary, IEP) stay inside the institution boundary at runtime. student-pii-redactor.kolm ships a typed detector for the FERPA-relevant span classes.

Institutional SSO
SAML & OIDC

SSO via Okta, InCommon Federation, Microsoft Entra, and Google Workspace. Service-account scoping per department, lab, or course. Audit log per tenant.

Research license
academic compile terms

Free academic compile for non-commercial research, plus a citation-ready model card. Weight files are open and forkable so the lab's reproducibility statement holds.

COPPA & state K-12
aligned

K-12 deployments need parental consent and data-minimization wrappers. The district procurement checklist and the Student Data Privacy Consortium (SDPC) addendum template are part of the education procurement packet.

02Use cases

Four reference workloads for academic and K-12 deployments.

Each is a signed .kolm artifact compiled against the institution's own gold set. The lab keeps the weights, the receipts, and the right to audit every output.

TA support triage.

Inbound student questions classified against the course rubric and routed (TA queue · office hours · self-serve answer). Suggested replies are drafted on the TA workstation, never round-tripped to a frontier API.

cookbook / support-reply →

Research-paper summarization.

Lab compiles a domain-tuned summarizer on its own corpus (PubMed, arXiv subfield, conference proceedings). Citations stay grounded, receipts let collaborators verify the exact artifact version.

cookbook / incident-summarizer →

Syllabus content moderation.

Flags syllabus-violating content in submitted assignments (plagiarism patterns, prohibited collaboration, scope creep). Reviewer-of-record signs every flag, kolm logs the artifact version.

cookbook / nps-classifier →

On-device tutor distillation.

Course-specific tutor distilled from approved instructor examples, deployed as a single signed file to the student tablet. Runs offline. No student data leaves the device.

03Reference artifact

One signed starting point.

A reference artifact your lab can fork, recompile against your own gold set, and cite in the methods section.

student-pii-redactor.kolm in development K 0.95 target

Typed detector for FERPA-relevant span classes (student name, ID, grade, IEP class, disciplinary text) with stable replacement tokens so downstream models train on shape, not raw PII. Recipe pattern at phi-redactor.
