Where they complement

It is fine to build your own.

Forking kolm is a feature, not a failure mode.

Fork the parts you need, ship the rest

The receipt schema, the manifest format, the distill recipes, and the verify CLI are all open. If you want only one piece — for example, the receipt format on top of your existing vLLM deployment — pull it out. We document the integration paths.

Most teams find the savings are in not reinventing the glue: the quant ladder, the eval recipes, and the offline verifier are the parts that take months to build right.
