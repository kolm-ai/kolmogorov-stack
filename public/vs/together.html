Where they complement

Pair them on purpose.

Many teams use Together for spiky hosted serving and Kolmogorov for steady-state self-hosted serving.

Hosted spillover ↔ self-hosted core

Use Kolmogorov to run your steady-state workload on your own GPUs at a fixed cost. Burst to Together (or any hosted provider) when traffic spikes past your owned capacity. The model is the same shape on both sides; receipts cover the self-hosted side.

Use Together's fine-tune as the teacher for a kolm distill run when you want the trained weights to move under your custody.
