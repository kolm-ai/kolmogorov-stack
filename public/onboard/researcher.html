Frontier teachers, frozen evals, reproducible artifacts

Switch path

Distill from a teacher

Pick a teacher, a base, and a recipe class. kolm runs the pipeline, generates seeds, distills, evaluates, and ships a signed artifact. Reproducible to the byte.

$ kolm distill \ --teacher deepseek-v4-flash \ --base qwen3-vl-7b \ --class extraction \ --seeds 1000 \ --eval external_holdout generating 1000 seeds (deterministic, seeded RNG). distilling at K-score gate 0.85. running 12-axis K-score. artifact OK. K-score: 0.91. composite gate cleared. external_holdout_hash bound. tenant_shadow_hash recorded. $ kolm verify my-artifact.kolm signature OK. receipt chain intact.

read more

Full distillation docs at /docs/distill. Recipe classes at /taxonomy. Frontier methods survey at /research/methods-2026-q2.

Submit to the K-score leaderboard

The kolm-bench v1 suite is 30 frozen cases across classification, extraction, generation, and code. Submit your artifact; the leaderboard re-runs the suite deterministically.

$ kolm bench --artifact my-artifact.kolm loading kolm-bench v1 (30 frozen cases). running 12-axis K-score per case. composite_mean: 0.918. pass: 27. fail: 3. $ kolm bench --artifact my-artifact.kolm --submit uploaded. leaderboard rank: 7 of 42. view at https://kolm.ai/kscore-leaderboard

read more

The leaderboard at /kscore-leaderboard, the methodology at /k-score, the bench spec at /kscore-bench.
