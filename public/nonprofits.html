kolm for mission-driven work

Free Pro for non-profits and academia

If you are a 501(c)(3) non-profit, an accredited university, an academic research lab, or a verified student researcher, the full kolm Pro tier is on us. Same artifact format, same K-score gate, same signed receipts, same local runtime. The only thing different is the invoice.

Who qualifies

If you fit any of the four buckets below, apply and we will verify and turn on Pro within 48 hours.

Non-profits

501(c)(3) registered organizations in the United States, plus EU equivalents: registered charities in the UK, gemeinnuetzige GmbH or e.V. in Germany, associations loi 1901 d'utilité publique in France, ONLUS or APS in Italy, fundaciones de interés general in Spain, and stichtingen in the Netherlands.

Academic institutions

Accredited universities, colleges, and research institutes worldwide. Includes public, private, and national-lab affiliated. Departmental labs and individual PIs both qualify.

Research labs

Independent academic research labs and consortia working on open-publication research, where outputs are released as papers, datasets, or open-source code.

Student researchers

Graduate students, postdocs, and undergraduate researchers with a verified .edu (or national equivalent) email and a brief description of the research project. PhD candidates and thesis work both count.

What you get

All Pro features. No watermarks, no degraded models, no “academic edition” gotchas.

100k / mo

Compiles per month. One compile = one signed .kolm artifact through the K-score gate.

10k / mo

Captured prompt/response pairs per month, retained in your private namespace.

All Pro

Private registry, drift detection, supersession chains, full receipt chain, K-score on every artifact.

Priority

Priority support queue. Compile-queue priority during peak times.

Fill in the five fields. We will verify your institution and email you within 48 hours.

Common questions

Is this really the same Pro tier?

Yes. Same model selection, same compile pipeline, same K-score gate, same signed-receipt chain, same private registry. No watermarks. No model gating.

What if I am at a for-profit research lab?

Then this program is not for you. Pro is $49/mo on the standard pricing page. Academic affiliation with a for-profit parent (e.g. industry-funded postdocs at a university) still qualifies under the university line.

Do I have to publish my results?

No. We do not require any specific publication outcome. We do ask that, where appropriate, you cite kolm in the methods section of any paper that uses a compiled artifact.

How long does the free Pro tier last?

For as long as your affiliation lasts. Students transition to a discounted alumni rate after graduation; non-profits renew annually with a single confirmation email.

Want the paid tiers instead?

If your organization does not fit the four buckets above, see standard pricing.
