Compliance-grade AI for moving money.

kolm.ai compiles approved fintech tasks into signed .kolm binaries that run inside your PCI scope, your BaaS sponsor's boundary, or your own VPC. Customer PII and transaction data never round-trip to a frontier API. Every output ships with a manifest, an eval set, and a tamper-evident receipt the examiner can re-verify.

Distinct from /finance: large-bank model-risk and trading-desk patterns live there. /fintech is for fast-iterating embedded-AI fintechs shipping product weekly.

01Regulatory framing

Controls your examiner already reads.

Each control below is described as the kolm posture today. We name the Wave marker for every control so your sponsor's compliance team can cite the artifact.

GLBA & SAR retention
aligned

SAR-relevant outputs persist into your retained log with the artifact version, the input hash, and the K-score. Five-year retention on receipt chains is configurable. The FinCEN exam-readiness checklist is part of the finance compliance packet.

PCI scope reduction
on-prem inference

Cardholder data never leaves the CDE because the artifact runs inside it. Vendor risk on the AI dependency drops from "third-party processor" to "signed dependency under change control."

receipt chain
