ISO 27001:2022 Annex A controls map.

Annex A of ISO 27001:2022 lists 93 controls across four families. This page is the operator-facing binding: thirty controls already satisfied by a kolm module, with the exact source path so the auditor can read the proof. Real Annex A IDs only. Stamped w767-v1.

Four control families

ISO 27001:2022 reorganized the prior 114 controls into four families (Annex A § 5 through § 8). The kolm stack ships controls across all four; blanks are scope items the auditor verifies elsewhere (for example, A.7 Physical security is largely a facilities concern rather than a kolm module).

Organizational 8 Annex A § 5 — policy, threat intel, access control, cloud, continuity.
People 2 Annex A § 6 — security awareness, event reporting.
Physical 1 Annex A § 7 — cloud-provider control (Vercel + Railway SOC 2).
Technological 19 Annex A § 8 — the meat: crypto, logging, masking, vuln mgmt, change.

Audit retention & continuous monitoring
