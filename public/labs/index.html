What kolm labs funds

Four research themes.

Concrete, reproducible work on the efficiency stack. If it ships as code, weights, a paper, or a public benchmark, we want to see it.

  • Efficient distillation methods

    teacher-student, on-policy, preference

    Novel student-architectures, smarter teacher trace selection, on-policy distill loops, distill-from-preference. Anything that pushes the compute-cost-vs-quality frontier on a published eval pack.

  • Novel quantization

    sub-4-bit, MoE-aware, calibration

    Sub-4-bit schemes, MoE-aware quantization, smarter calibration sets, mixed-precision discovery. We care about KL-divergence retention against the BF16 base, not headline bit counts.

  • Multimodal distill

    vision, audio, video, sensor

    Distill vision-language, speech, and time-series teachers down to artifacts a phone or an embedded board can actually run. Bonus points for honest empty-state handling when modality coverage is incomplete.

  • On-device inference benchmarks

    phones, laptops, edge, embedded

    Reproducible, signed benchmarks on real consumer and edge hardware. Pixel, iPhone, M-series Mac, Jetson, Raspberry Pi, microcontrollers. We will publish your runs alongside their receipts.

What we offer

Compute, hours, credit. No equity. No IP claim.

A grant is meant to unblock real work. The terms reflect that. You keep your code, your weights, your paper, and your name on the work.

  • Compute credits

    $1,000 - $10,000 per project

    Teacher-API budget on the kolm Gateway. Use it to run captures, distill loops, and bake-offs against frontier teachers without spending your own balance.

  • kolm Cloud GPU hours

    H100 / B200 / 5090-class

    Scheduled time on kolm Cloud GPUs for distill, quantize, and inference benchmarking. Hours scale with the scope you propose in your application.

  • Co-authorship on outcomes

    if relevant

    If the work becomes a published paper or kolm blog post and you want us listed, we will. If you would rather we stay off the author line, that is fine too. Your call.

  • No equity, no IP claim

    author keeps their work

    We do not take equity in your lab or company. We do not claim IP over what you build with the grant. License your output however you like; we only ask that the receipts you generate on kolm stay verifiable.
