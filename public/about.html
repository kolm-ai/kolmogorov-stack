About kolm.ai

An open-source compiler for AI.

We build the chain from your prompts to a signed artifact your compliance team can verify. Apache-2.0. Self-hostable. Runs air-gapped.

Kolm is a small team building an open-source AI compiler. We capture the prompts your product already sends to a frontier model, distill them into a private specialist trained on your traffic, and compile that specialist into a signed .kolm artifact you can deploy to your own hardware — cloud, on-prem, edge, or air-gapped.

Everything we ship is in one Apache-2.0 repo. Every artifact carries an Ed25519 receipt. Every K-Score is reproducible from a content-addressed eval pack. The model that decides should be the model that gets audited — that line is the entire manifesto.

We exist because most teams pay per token for a model they cannot see, sign, or stand behind. We think you should pay for compute, not tokens, and that the artifact running the decision should be the artifact your compliance team approved.
