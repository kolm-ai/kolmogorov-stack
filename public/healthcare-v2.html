What clinical buyers tell us.

Concrete pains we hear from CMIOs, compliance leads, and ML platform teams.

PHI leaves the building

  • SaaS LLMs log prompts that carry names, MRNs, diagnoses
  • BAAs cover storage, not training-set inclusion
  • De-identification breaks on free-text notes

No way to replay a decision

  • Joint Commission auditors want deterministic outputs
  • Vendor models silently version-bump weekly
  • Re-running a six-month-old decision is impossible

Workflows differ per site

  • USCDI extraction, ED triage, and prior-auth letters vary per system
  • General models hallucinate ICD-10 and CPT codes
  • Fine-tuning needs PHI-safe pipelines you do not have

Architecture: PHI stays inside your tenant boundary your VPC.

Capture, compile, and inference all run in your tenant. The only thing kolm ships you is software and a signature key.
