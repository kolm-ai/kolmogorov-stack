Forge

Quantize a model.

Pick a base model and a target shortcut. We auto-fill the format, run a fit check against your local hardware, and queue the compile job. Backed by POST /v1/quantize; the format/quant mapping mirrors the kolm compile --target shortcut table.

CLI parity

Same path from the terminal: kolm compile --target gguf-q4km --base-model Qwen/Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct. Use kolm fit first to pre-check; kolm hardware for the supported-quants matrix on your box.
