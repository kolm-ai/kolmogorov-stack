Danger zone

Leave organization

You will lose access to all artifacts, keys, and shared resources in this org. The owner cannot leave; transfer ownership first.

Delete organization

This deletes the org, all of its API keys, captured calls, datasets, artifacts, and billing history. Receipts already issued remain verifiable. This cannot be undone.

Transfer ownership

The new owner must already be a member of this org. They become the sole owner; you become an admin. Logged to the audit ledger.
