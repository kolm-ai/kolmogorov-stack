Namespaces.

Each namespace is one logical workload. Drift is scored over a 7-day lookback against a 30-day baseline using three signals: fallback-rate delta, distribution distance, and volume ratio. Status uses a 2σ/3σ SPC ladder over baseline variance.

Data health

Coverage, balance, and freshness of the corpus feeding this namespace. The autopilot lifts each bar; the K-Score trend is the visible result.
