Chargeback.

Per-department, per-project, per-namespace cost attribution sourced from the authoritative event-store cost ledger. Exportable CSV (RFC 4180) + JSON for finance hand-off. Extends /account/billing from "totals per tenant" to "breakdown per dimension". Backed by src/chargeback.js (W783).
