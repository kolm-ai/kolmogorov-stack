Savings.

What kolm displaced this period. For each namespace we re-price the routed-to-local receipts against the frontier rate card you would have paid otherwise, then net out the one-time compile cost. Backed by GET /v1/savings/summary + GET /v1/savings/displacement.

Tenant-wide totals across the selected period. Numbers re-price each local receipt at the frontier model's published rate at the time it ran (no future repricing).

CLI mirror

Every action on this page has a CLI equivalent. Useful for scripting + CI.

# tenant-wide summary kolm savings --period 30d # per-namespace displacement (R-8) kolm savings --namespace support --period 30d # with explicit frontier baseline kolm savings --namespace support --period 30d \ --frontier-provider anthropic --frontier-model claude-sonnet-4-6 # export to CSV for the finance review kolm savings --period 30d --json | jq -r '...'

Methodology: displaced $ is sum of (local receipt count × frontier rate-card $/req). Net $ subtracts your actual local inference cost plus the amortized compile cost over the period. No fee math is layered in this view — for billing see Billing.
