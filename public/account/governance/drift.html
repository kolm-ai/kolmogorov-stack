Drift signals.

Three signals per namespace, with a 2σ warn / 3σ alert ladder over baseline variance. Rising fallback rate is the canonical "your traffic shifted away from the artifact" signal; distribution distance and volume ratio cross-check it.

Per-namespace status

Read /docs/govern/drift

How the ladder works

For each signal, the detector computes the mean and standard deviation over the baseline window. Status escalates as the live value crosses 2σ then 3σ from the baseline mean.

Recommended response

When a namespace tips into warn, kolm proposes a capture-priority list for the next re-distill. On alert, the recommendation is automatic: open the dataset, prioritize the drifted buckets, and re-compile.

kolm drift status --namespace support
kolm drift status --namespace support --json
kolm captures list --namespace support --drifted-only
kolm compile support --from latest-distillation --priority drifted
