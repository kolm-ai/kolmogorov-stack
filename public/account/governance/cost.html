Rate-card discipline

The frozen rate card lives in data/rate-card.json. Receipts older than that file are repriced using the version that was current at the time the receipt was signed. No retroactive savings inflation.

kolm savings --namespace support --period 30
kolm savings --namespace support --period 365 --json
kolm savings --cumulative
kolm rate-card show
