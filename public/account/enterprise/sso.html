2. Configure your identity provider

Upload your IdP metadata (URL preferred, XML accepted). Domains listed here will be auto-routed to SSO at sign-in. JIT provisioning creates users on first SSO sign-in with the default role.

3. Test sign-in

After saving, open /signin from a private window with an email matching one of the auto-routed domains. The login form will redirect to your IdP. Successful return lands you on /account/overview with a JIT-created session.
