Invite a teammate

Send an email invite. The recipient creates or signs into a kolm.ai account, accepts, and lands at /account/overview scoped to this organization. Invites expire after 14 days. Posts to POST /v1/orgs/:org_id/invites.

Enter the work email of the person you want to add. They'll get a one-time link from noreply@kolm.ai.

Only existing owners can grant the Owner role. See the role legend below for permission scope.

Role legend

Four roles, each with a scoped permission set. Full permission matrix is in the docs at /docs/rbac.

Owner Full control

Manage every setting, billing, members, BYOC, BAA, and tenancy. Can promote others to Owner. At least one Owner is required at all times. Removing the last Owner is blocked at the API.

Admin Operate the org

Invite and remove members, change roles (Member, Billing, Admin — not Owner), manage API keys, view billing. Cannot delete the organization or transfer ownership.

Member Build and operate

Read and write to artifacts, datasets, captures, deploys, and audit log entries scoped to this org. Cannot invite, remove, or change roles.

Billing Finance access

Read invoices, change payment method, view usage meters, download billing exports. No artifact, dataset, or capture access; no member management.

Remove this member?

They will lose access to the organization immediately. Their captures, datasets, and artifacts stay with the org. They can be re-invited later.
