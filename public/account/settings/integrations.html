Account/Settings/Integrations

Integrations.

Connect RunPod, Kubernetes, and other compute providers. Keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and scoped to your tenant. The "Test connection" button validates against the provider before save.

RP

RunPod

Serverless GPU pods · one-click deploy · pay-per-hour
Not configured

Provision a single GPU pod from a .kolm artifact in under 90 seconds. Get an OpenAI-compatible URL the moment the pod warms up. Billed directly by RunPod (we don't take a cut on GPU time).

Get a key at runpod.io/console/user/settings. Required permission: Read/Write for Pods.

K8

Kubernetes

Apply the kolm operator to your cluster · BYOC
Not configured

Paste a kubeconfig fragment (server URL + service-account token). The operator runs in the kolm-system namespace and reconciles KolmDeployment custom resources for each artifact you ship.

Generate a service-account token: kubectl create token kolm-deployer -n kolm-system. Required RBAC: create/get/list/delete on deployments, services, and kolmdeployments.kolm.ai.

M

Modal

Serverless function compute · cold-start in seconds
Not configured

Run the kolm compile/benchmark job on Modal. Pay per compute-second. Useful for short bursty workloads that don't justify a dedicated pod.

Find your token at modal.com/settings/tokens.
