Multimodal bakeoff.

Replay your captured multimodal events (image / audio / video / pdf) through each compiled .kolm artifact. Score each artifact's output against the captured base-model response by token-overlap (Jaccard). Highest mean score wins.

Contestants

Source: src/multimodal-bakeoff.js. Tenant-fenced. Pure string/token scoring, no heavy ML in router. Embedding-similarity scoring is opt-in via KOLM_MULTIMODAL_SCORE_CMD worker hook.
