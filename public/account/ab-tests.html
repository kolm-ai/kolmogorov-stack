W777 traffic splitting · W778 statistical-significance gate

A/B tests.

Split traffic deterministically (FNV-1a hash on request_hash) between two artifact versions, record the K-Score and latency on each outcome, then watch the W778 Welch t-test decide pass / fail / insufficient. Auto-rollback fires when the promoted arm B underperforms arm A on the gate. Every decision lands in the audit log under workflow_id=w777:ab. See /docs/releasing for the release procedure.
