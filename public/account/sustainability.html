Cumulative CO2 saved (.kolm local vs cloud round-trip)

Difference between (a) the energy cost of serving captured calls from the distilled .kolm on local hardware and (b) the estimated energy cost of round-tripping the same calls to a frontier cloud API. Positive numbers mean local was greener; negative numbers mean the cloud was greener for that workload.
