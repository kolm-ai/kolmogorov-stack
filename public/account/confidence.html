Mid-response teacher splice telemetry

Confidence routing.

watches a streaming entropy window during student generation and splices in the teacher mid-response when the window crosses your threshold. This page reports the local-vs-teacher ratio, cost saved, splice latency percentiles, and the distribution of thresholds in use.
