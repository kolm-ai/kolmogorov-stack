SLA tier mapping

Which tiers carry which commitments.

Free is best-effort by design -- it is a sandbox tier and we will not promise nines we cannot keep on a sandbox. Paid tiers carry contractual commitments with credit mechanics.

Tier Uptime commitment Latency commitment Credit mechanic
Free best-effort none none
Pro 99.5 percent p95 documented service credit at 99.0 percent breach
Business 99.9 percent p95 and p99 documented service credit at 99.5 percent breach
Enterprise 99.95 percent p95, p99, p99.9 documented contractual credits at 99.9 percent breach
