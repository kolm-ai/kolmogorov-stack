Start a distill job.

Tell us what task you want a specialist for, point at the namespace where your captures live, and we'll plan a strategy from your real-pair count, privacy mode, and configured teachers. The recommended strategy is what we'd run from kolm distill with the same inputs.

3. After submit

Distill jobs run on the worker pool. Watch progress on /account/distill-runs — each run drops progress.jsonl + a final .kolm artifact that lands in /account/artifacts. From the CLI: kolm distill runs <run_id>.

Need to upload a custom corpus first? /account/datasets has the upload form. Need to pick a different base / teacher / hardware? Use kolm distill --help for the full flag set.
