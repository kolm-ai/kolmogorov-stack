Provider keys.

Store the provider keys your team already pays for. Their AI traffic routes through kolm under the key, so every call is captured, attributed, and kept in your team lake. Keys are encrypted at rest and never shown again after you save them.

A member key is yours alone. A team key is usable by everyone on the team and only an admin can rotate or remove it. Either way, the raw key never leaves the vault and never appears in a receipt or a log.

Add a provider key

For a team key you must be a team admin. The value field is write-only; saving stores it encrypted and clears the field.

Point a client at kolm

Once a key is stored, set the base URL of any OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, or OpenRouter client to kolm and keep using it as normal. kolm routes the call under the stored key and records the request and response in your team lake.
