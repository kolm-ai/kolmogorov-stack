Runtime Targets

Per-(runtime, target) capability fingerprint produced by ExportForge for each format. tested rows are real measurements; estimated rows come from a compile-time model; unsupported rows mark incompatible combinations and link to a recommended fallback.

Trust packet

Procurement-ready bundle of claims (data provenance, model integrity, deployment integrity, drift monitoring) and framework controls (SOC 2, HIPAA, EU AI Act, ISO 27001) with evidence pointers. Hand this to a buyer's risk team to short-circuit a months-long questionnaire cycle.

No artifacts to export. Compile one first.

Evidence

Per-artifact provenance graph: capture, eval, teacher, student, runtime, signature, policy, rights nodes joined by derived_from, validated_by, invalidates, supersedes edges. Revoking a node marks every descendant as needs_review - receipts stay valid, but downstream artifacts surface a review flag until re-attested.

No artifacts with an evidence DAG yet. Build with capture + eval evidence to populate this panel.
