Opportunities.

The opportunity engine watches your captures and surfaces 11 kinds of savings. Accept to wire it up, dismiss to silence it.

Opportunity legend (11 types)

TypeWhat it means
cache_candidateIdentical request hash repeated. Cache the response.
cheaper_model_candidateFrontier model used for short or structured replies. A cheap model would suffice.
local_replacement_candidateTemplate signature seen 100+ times. Compile a local artifact.
privacy_leakSensitive class detected under an allow policy. Tighten the policy.
prompt_compressionLong prompts share a consistent prefix. Move it to a system message.
repeated_extractionJSON output with consistent schema. Distill an extraction recipe.
repeated_classificationOutput in a small enumerated set. Classifier replacement candidate.
log_triagePrompts about logs or errors with categorical output. Triage classifier.
routing_policySame prompt type sent to multiple providers. Pick one or define a router.
dataset_ready1,000+ events with strong template clustering. Build a dataset.
training_readyDataset ready and a candidate holdout exists. Kick off training.
TypeReasonSavingRiskCalls
Nothing surfaced yet. Opportunities show up once the engine has enough signal, usually 50+ events per namespace. Send more AI calls through the capture proxy and re-check.
