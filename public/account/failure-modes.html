Tenant-wide stream.

W812 buckets your captures by topic + length + vendor class, then surfaces the clusters where the student (compiled artifact) diverges most from the teacher (upstream provider). The largest regressions feed the W720 self-improvement loop as weakness signals so the worst clusters re-distill first.

Honesty note. Clusters are a CHEAP heuristic (char-3-gram + length bucket + vendor class) — not a learned embedding. If you need finer-grained topics, run workers/failure-clustering/ (W812 isolation pattern).
