Active learning loop (W710 queue + W815 coverage gaps)

Active learning loop.

Captures the student couldn't handle locally become next round's training signal. The router escalates to the teacher, the row lands in the queue below, and the next distill picks it up via kolm distill --resume-from-active-queue. Below the queue, the W815 coverage-gap heatmap surfaces topic clusters that are under-represented vs production traffic, so the next distill can fill the gap.

Coverage-gap heatmap (W815 active-learning loop)

Topic clusters under-represented relative to the corpus median, ranked by gap_score = shortfall × demand_proxy.
