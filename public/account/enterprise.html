Audit & retention

Audit log retention is 365 days on Business; Enterprise can extend to 7 years (HIPAA §164.316(b)(2)(i)) and stream events to your SIEM. Hash-chained — tampering is detectable.

Retention window 365d

Default 365 days. Configurable on Enterprise.

SIEM streaming not configured

Stream every audit event via webhook to Splunk, Datadog, Sumo Logic, or a custom HTTPS endpoint.

Tenant export portable

Full tenant dump including audit log: kolm export-tenant --out tenant.tar.gz. No vendor lock-in.

Tenant deletion irreversible

Right-to-deletion honored within 30 days per GDPR Art 17. Receipts (cryptographic proofs) retained as anchored hashes only.

Procurement & vendor-security

Pre-answered SIG Lite + CSA CAIQ v4.0.2. Drop into your procurement workflow. Generated from data/procurement/*.json — same source the CLI uses.

# list available frameworks kolm procurement list # Shared Assessments SIG Lite — markdown kolm procurement sig --out kolm-sig.md # CSA CAIQ v4.0.2 — JSON for upload kolm procurement caiq --format json --out kolm-caiq.json # both bundled, CSV for vendor portal upload kolm procurement all --format csv --out vendor-pack.csv
