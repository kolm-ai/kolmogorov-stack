Forge

Merge two artifacts.

Combine two compiled .kolm artifacts into one. Same-lineage required (same base model) for most methods. Dry-run prints the plan, heuristic K-Score delta, and surfaces a base-model mismatch warning. Backed by POST /v1/merge.

CLI parity

Same path: kolm merge base.kolm head.kolm --method ties --dry-run. Drop --dry-run to write the merged artifact. Method choice is hardcoded against the same heuristic K-Score deltas this page renders.
