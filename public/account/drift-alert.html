Drift alert.

Frontier AI on your own infrastructure. KL / Jensen-Shannon divergence between your training capture distribution and today's production traffic. When JSD crosses the alert threshold a webhook fires AND the next W709 routing decision is stamped drift_warning:true. Built by src/drift-alert.js (W747). See /docs/drift-alert for the math.

Suggestions

Plain-English shift suggestions, sorted by relative growth. Each suggestion is actionable: capture more examples of the named token until JSD returns under threshold.

Webhook config

Register a webhook URL that fires (POST) when an alert is detected. Payload: {kolm_alert:'distribution_shift', namespace, jsd, top_diverging, suggestions, ts}. Best-effort delivery, 5s timeout, no retries.
