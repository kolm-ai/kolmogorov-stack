Clinical workflows we see in production

Patterns kolm runs today.

Every workflow ships with defer-on-low-confidence and a human gate by default. Turning either off is a deliberate switch.

Ambulatory

SOAP-note structuring

Turns transcribed clinical narratives into structured SOAP against your in-house template. EHR write is human-confirmed only.

input: transcript
output: SOAP {S,O,A,P}
EHR-write: human-confirmed
Revenue cycle

Prior-auth assembly

Reads chart + payer policy, drafts the prior-auth packet, cites the supporting evidence by chart locator. Human reviews + submits.

input: chart + payer-rule
output: prior-auth-draft + cites
cite-required: yes
Radiology / triage

Worklist prioritization

Reads DICOM headers only (pixel-level access opt-in), reorders the radiologist worklist by clinical urgency, defers ambiguity.

input: dicom-header
output: priority {high, normal, low}
defer: low-confidence
Research

Cohort assembly

Reads protocol inclusion / exclusion criteria, queries the EHR for matches, returns a ranked list with per-patient citations. PHI stays inside the enclave throughout.

input: protocol + EHR query
output: cohort + per-patient cites
residency: in-VPC
Member-facing

Nurse-line triage

Triage chatbot distilled from your nurse-line transcripts. Routes to self-care / nurse / ED. Escalation is always available.

input: member-message
output: route {self-care, nurse, ED}
escalate: always-available
Population health

Care-gap outreach

Reads quality-measure denominators, drafts in brand voice, hands the queue to a coordinator with full context attached. Send is human-approved.

input: HEDIS gap + member
output: outreach + brief
send: human-approved
