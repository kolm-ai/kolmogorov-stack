Use OpenAI for general intelligence on demand. Use kolm when a repeated task needs to become a signed artifact you own, audit, and run without renting every inference.

When OpenAI is the right answer.

You need general reasoning. Your task changes shape weekly. You don't run the same prompt a million times. Volume is low and a per-call bill is fine. Or you're at the prototyping stage and time-to-first-answer beats unit economics. Use the API.

When kolm is the right answer.

You have a task, redact PHI, classify support tickets, extract invoice fields, translate medical instructions, score a credit decision, that runs at scale and the answer must be reproducible. Compile once: ship the artifact, get the receipt, run it on your hardware, audit every call. The frontier is the teacher; the artifact is the worker.
