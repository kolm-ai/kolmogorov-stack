kolm vs Replicate

kolm vs Replicate: which should you pick?

Replicate runs community models behind a pay-per-prediction API; kolm compiles your traffic into a model you own with no per-prediction bill. Pick Replicate for instant access to a catalog of hosted models. Pick kolm when you want to own and quantize a specialist trained on your own workload.

When to pick each

Choose Replicate when you want one-line access to a huge catalog of community models for prototyping or low-volume use, with zero infrastructure to manage. FAQ

Can kolm import a Replicate model?
kolm compiles open-weight base models and distills specialists from your captured traffic. It does not re-host closed catalog endpoints; instead you point your client at the kolm gateway and build from your own pairs.
Is there a free tier?
Yes, kolm has a free self-serve tier. Sign up to obtain an API key and compile your first artifact.
What about reproducibility?
Compiling the same inputs on the same hardware yields identical output hashes, and the receipt makes that auditable.

Try it

How do I start with kolm?

Sign up for a free kolm key, point your existing OpenAI-compatible client at the gateway base URL, and let it capture traffic. When the lake is full, run kolm distill then kolm compile to produce a signed, quantized model you own. Every build is replayable at /verify-prod, so you can verify before you ship.
