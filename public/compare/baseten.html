kolm vs Baseten

kolm vs Baseten: which should you pick?

Baseten packages and autoscales your model deployments in its cloud; kolm compiles a signed, portable model you own and run anywhere. Pick Baseten for managed deployment ergonomics. Pick kolm when ownership, portability, and a verifiable build receipt matter more than a hosted control plane.

FAQ

Does kolm package models like Truss?
kolm's package is the .kolm artifact: quantized weights plus a signed receipt and the original weight hashes. It loads in standard runtimes (llama.cpp, vLLM, ExLlamaV2) without a kolm dependency.
Can I deploy a kolm artifact to my own cloud?
Yes. kolm deploy pushes an artifact to a device or VPC you control. There is no kolm control plane in the inference path.
How do I verify a build?
Run kolm verify on the artifact, or replay the receipt CID at /verify-prod. It checks the SHA-256 weight chain, the Ed25519 signature, and the K-score.
